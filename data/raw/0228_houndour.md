## Identity & Lore
Houndour (Pokedex #228) is the Dark Pokémon, a dual-type Dark/Fire canine introduced in Generation II (Johto). Recognized by its black fur, skull-like plating on its forehead, and skeletal rib ridges, this quadrupedal Pokémon is a pack hunter found primarily in rough terrain. Houndour is highly social within its species, utilizing a complex array of distinct cries to communicate with pack members and coordinate pursuit strategies to corner prey. While it is the first stage of its line, its evolution possesses the capability to Mega Evolve into Mega Houndoom.

## Combat Profile
*   **Stats:** HP 45, Attack 60, Defense 30, Sp. Atk 80, Sp. Def 50, Speed 65.
*   **Archetype:** Special Wallbreaker / Glass Cannon.
*   **Type Matchups:** As a Dark/Fire type, it is **weak to Fighting, Ground, Rock, and Water**. It possesses a distinct tactical advantage with an **immunity to Psychic** attacks and **resistances to Ghost, Steel, Grass, Ice, Dark, and Fire**.
*   **Move Selection:** With a significantly higher Special Attack (80) than Attack, Houndour excels when utilizing **Dark Pulse** and **Flamethrower** (or **Fire Blast**) for STAB (Same Type Attack Bonus). It can boost its offensive pressure further with **Nasty Plot**, while **Sludge Bomb** provides essential coverage against Fairy-types.
*   **Abilities:**
    *   **Early Bird:** The Pokémon wakes up from sleep status twice as fast as usual.
    *   **Flash Fire:** Grants immunity to Fire-type moves and boosts the power of its own Fire moves by 50% when hit by one, making it an excellent switch-in against Fire attacks.
    *   **Unnerve (Hidden):** Unnerves opposing Pokémon and makes them unable to eat Berries.

## Evolution & Biology
Houndour is the first stage of a two-stage evolutionary line. It evolves into **Houndoom** starting at **Level 24**. It belongs to the **Ground (Field)** Egg Group and has a base hatch counter of 20 cycles. Houndour are strictly organized by a hierarchy within their packs, obeying the orders of the senior-most member without question.
