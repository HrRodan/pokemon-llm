## Identity & Lore
Ponyta (Pokedex #077) is the **Fire Horse Pok√©mon** introduced in **Generation I (Kanto)**. This quadrupedal creature is characterized by its cream-colored coat and a mane and tail composed of burning flames. Inhabiting vast **grasslands**, Ponyta is celebrated for its incredible jumping ability and diamond-hard hooves, which are capable of trampling obstacles flat. While the standard form is a pure Fire-type, a Psychic-type regional variant known as **Galarian Ponyta** exists in the Galar region, identifiable by its cotton-candy-like mane.

## Combat Profile
*   **Stats:** HP 50, Attack 85, Defense 55, Sp. Atk 65, Sp. Def 65, Speed 90.
*   **Archetype:** Fast Physical Attacker / Glass Cannon.
*   **Type Matchups:** As a pure Fire-type, it is **weak to Water, Ground, and Rock**. It **resists Fire, Grass, Ice, Bug, Steel, and Fairy** moves.
*   **Move Selection:** Capitalizing on its high Speed and Attack, key moves include **Flare Blitz** (primary STAB), **High Horsepower** (coverage against Rock/Electric), **Wild Charge** (coverage against Water), and **Morning Sun** (sustain) or **Will-O-Wisp** (to cripple physical attackers).
*   **Abilities:**
    *   **Run Away:** Guarantees escape from wild battles.
    *   **Flash Fire:** Grants immunity to Fire-type moves and boosts the power of its own Fire moves when hit by one.
    *   **Flame Body (Hidden):** Has a 30% chance to burn the opponent when hit by a contact move.

## Evolution & Biology
Ponyta is the first stage of a two-stage evolutionary line. It evolves into **Rapidash** starting at **Level 40**. It belongs to the **Field** (listed as Ground in data) Egg Group and requires approximately 20 egg cycles to hatch. Biologically, a newborn Ponyta struggles to stand but strengthens its legs rapidly by chasing after its parents.
