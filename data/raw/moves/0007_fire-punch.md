## Identity & Mechanics
**Fire Punch** is a Physical Fire-type move introduced in Generation I.
* **Description:** The user focuses intense thermal energy into its fist, unleashing a punch wreathed in searing flames. This "Elemental Punch" is a staple of physical combatants, combining martial prowess with elemental combustion to strike the target.
* **Effect:** Inflicts regular damage to a single target.
* **Secondary Effect:** 10% chance to Burn the target.
* **Targeting:** Single Pokémon.

## Competitive Profile
* **Stats:** Power 75, Accuracy 100%, PP 15 (Max 24).
* **Role:** Consistency Option / Physical Coverage.
* **Usage:** While it lacks the sheer wallbreaking power of **Flare Blitz**, Fire Punch is favored for its lack of recoil and perfect accuracy. It serves as an essential coverage move for Physical attackers to bypass **Steel, Bug, Grass, and Ice** types. It is particularly valuable on Pokémon with the **Iron Fist** ability (e.g., Ledian, Golurk, Conkeldurr), which boosts its power by 20%.
* **Attributes:** This is a **Contact** move and a **Punch-based** move, making it interact with items like Rocky Helmet and abilities like Iron Barbs or Tough Claws.

## Distribution & Notable Users
It has a **Wide** distribution (209 learners), making it one of the most accessible elemental coverage moves in the franchise.
* **Prominent Users:** Iconic STAB (Same Type Attack Bonus) users include **Charizard (specifically Mega Charizard X)**, **Blaziken**, **Infernape**, and **Emboar**.
* **Coverage Utility:** Frequently seen on non-Fire Pokémon like **Dragonite**, **Tyranitar**, and **Machamp** to counter 4x Fire-weak threats like Scizor or Ferrothorn. It is also a common choice for Paradox Pokémon like **Iron Hands**.
* **Acquisition:** Widely available via **TM** (e.g., TM05 in Gen 8/9) and historically through Move Tutors in almost every generation.
