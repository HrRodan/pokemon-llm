## Identity & Mechanics
**Poison Jab** is a Physical Poison-type move introduced in Generation IV (Sinnoh).
* **Description:** The user thrusts a toxin-drenched limb, tentacle, or weapon into the target, delivering a precise and viscous strike. This move represents a calculated biological attack, using the user's physical reach to inject potent venom directly into the opponent's bloodstream.
* **Effect:** Inflicts regular physical damage.
* **Secondary Effect:** Has a high **30% chance** to induce the **Poison** status condition.
* **Targeting:** Selected single target.

## Competitive Profile
* **Stats:** Power 80, Accuracy 100%, PP 20 (Max 32).
* **Role:** **Reliable STAB** / **Anti-Fairy Coverage**.
* **Usage:** Poison Jab is the gold standard for Physical Poison-type consistency. While *Gunk Shot* offers significantly higher Power (120), its shaky accuracy (80%) often makes Poison Jab the preferred choice for reliable KOs. Since the introduction of the Fairy-type in Generation VI, Poison Jab has transformed from a niche move into a vital coverage tool for Physical attackers to bypass defensive Fairy-types. It deals **Super Effective** damage to **Grass** and **Fairy** types.
* **Attributes:** This is a **Contact** move, meaning it triggers effects like Rocky Helmet, Rough Skin, or Iron Barbs.
