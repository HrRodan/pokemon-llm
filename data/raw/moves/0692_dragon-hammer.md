## Identity & Mechanics
**Dragon Hammer** is a Physical Dragon-type move introduced in Generation VII (Alola).
* **Description:** The user utilizes its massive body or elongated neck like a heavy mallet, swinging with immense force to crush the target. This draconic strike emphasizes blunt force trauma and physical weight, delivering a high-impact blow without the recoil or drawbacks often associated with powerful Dragon-type attacks.
* **Effect:** Inflicts consistent, regular damage to a single target.
* **Secondary Effect:** None (0% chance).
* **Targeting:** Selected Pokémon (Single).

## Competitive Profile
* **Stats:** Power 90, Accuracy 100%, PP 15.
* **Role:** Reliable STAB / Consistency Option.
* **Usage:** Dragon Hammer serves as a direct upgrade to **Dragon Claw** (80 Power) for those who can learn it, offering a more potent offensive presence without the locking mechanic or confusion drawback of **Outrage**. While it lacks the raw power of Outrage or the Draco Meteor nuke, its perfect accuracy and solid base power make it a premium Physical STAB option for maintaining offensive pressure. It deals **Super Effective** damage to **Dragon** types.
* **Attributes:** Makes **Contact**.

## Distribution & Notable Users
It has a **very narrow** distribution (3 total learners), making it a semi-exclusive move associated with specific body types.
* **Prominent Users:** Primarily utilized by **Alolan Exeggutor** (its signature user upon debut) and the Paradox Pokémon **Raging Bolt**, both of whom utilize their long necks to deliver the "hammer" blow. 
* **Coverage Utility:** Also learned by **Tropius**, providing the Grass/Flying type with a unique Physical Dragon-type coverage option to hit Dragon-type threats that would otherwise resist its STABs.
* **Acquisition:** Extremely limited availability; it is **not** available as a TM or TR in any generation, restricted entirely to natural level-up or move reminder sets for its specific users.
