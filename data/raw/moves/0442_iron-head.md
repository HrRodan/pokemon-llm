## Identity & Mechanics
**Iron Head** is a Physical Steel-type move introduced in Generation IV (Sinnoh region).
* **Description:** The user hardens its head to a metallic density and delivers a powerful, blunt-force ramming attack. This high-impact collision not only deals significant physical damage but frequently staggers the opponent, leaving them momentarily dazed and unable to act.
* **Effect:** Inflicts regular damage and possesses a high probability of causing the target to flinch, provided the user moves before the target.
* **Secondary Effect:** **30%** chance to cause the target to **Flinch**.
* **Targeting:** Selected Single Target.

## Competitive Profile
* **Stats:** Power 80, Accuracy 100%, PP 15 (Max 24).
* **Role:** Reliable STAB / Consistency Option.
* **Usage:** Iron Head is the gold standard for Physical Steel-type offense due to its perfect accuracy and disruptive flinch chance. It is frequently paired with Speed control (like Thunder Wave or Choice Scarf) to create "Para-Flinch" strategies, effectively locking an opponent out of their turn. It is statistically superior to *Steel Wing* and more reliable than the weight-dependent *Heavy Slam* or the accuracy-challenged *Meteor Mash*. It deals **Super Effective** damage to **Ice, Rock, and Fairy** types.
* **Attributes:** Makes **Contact**. This move is particularly vital in modern competitive play as a primary tool for "Fairy-slaying," checking top-tier threats like Clefable or Flutter Mane.

It has a **Wide** distribution (286 learners), making it one of the most accessible Steel-type physical attacks in the franchise.
* **Prominent Users:** Iconic STAB users include **Metagross**, **Excadrill**, **Kingambit**, and **Zacian-Crowned**. It is also the signature weapon of **Jirachi**, which utilizes its *Serene Grace* ability to boost the flinch chance to a devastating 60%.
