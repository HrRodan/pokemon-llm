## Identity & Mechanics
**Shadow Ball** is a Special Ghost-type move introduced in Generation II.
* **Description:** The user materializes a globule of condensed, dark spectral energy and hurls it at the target. This swirling orb of shadows resonates with malice, often weakening the target's spiritual and mental defenses upon impact.
* **Effect:** Inflicts regular damage and has a significant chance to compromise the target's durability.
* **Secondary Effect:** 20% chance to lower the targetâ€™s Special Defense by one stage.
* **Targeting:** Selected target (Single).

## Competitive Profile
* **Stats:** Power 80, Accuracy 100%, PP 15 (Max 24).
* **Role:** Reliable STAB / Consistency Option.
* **Usage:** Shadow Ball is the gold standard for Special Ghost-type offense. While its base power is moderate compared to moves like Fire Blast or Hydro Pump, its perfect accuracy and the high-utility 20% Sp. Def drop make it highly spammable. It deals **Super Effective** damage to **Ghost** and **Psychic** types. In competitive play, it provides near-unresisted neutral coverage alongside Fighting-type moves (the "Ghost/Fighting" coverage core).
* **Attributes:** This is a **Ball/Pulse** move and is notably blocked by the Ability **Bulletproof**.

## Distribution & Notable Users
It has an exceptionally wide distribution (396 learners), making it one of the most accessible Special coverage moves in the game.
* **Prominent Users:** It is a mandatory STAB (Same Type Attack Bonus) tool for elite threats like **Gengar**, **Flutter Mane**, **Gholdengo**, and **Dragapult**. 
* **Coverage Utility:** Frequently equipped by high-tier Psychic and Normal types such as **Alakazam**, **Mewtwo**, and **Blissey** to provide a reliable answer to opposing Ghost and Psychic-type threats that would otherwise wall their primary attacks.
* **Acquisition:** Extremely common; it has been available as a **TM** (Technical Machine) or **TR** (Technical Record) in every generation since its debut.
