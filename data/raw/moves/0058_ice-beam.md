## Identity & Mechanics
**Ice Beam** is a Special Ice-type move introduced in Generation I.
* **Description:** The user fires a concentrated, sub-zero ray of freezing energy from its mouth or body at the target. This cryogenic blast is known for its ability to flash-freeze opponents, making it one of the most feared elemental attacks in competitive play.
* **Effect:** Inflicts consistent Special damage.
* **Secondary Effect:** 10% chance to **Freeze** the target, a potentially game-ending status condition that completely prevents the victim from taking action.
* **Targeting:** Single Target.

## Competitive Profile
* **Stats:** Power 90, Accuracy 100%, PP 10 (Max 16).
* **Role:** Reliable STAB / Premier Coverage.
* **Usage:** Ice Beam is considered the "Gold Standard" of Ice-type moves. It is favored over *Blizzard* for its perfect accuracy and over *Icy Wind* for its superior damage output. It deals **Super Effective** damage to **Grass, Ground, Flying,** and **Dragon** types. It is historically the primary tool used to **Check** or **Nuke** 4x Ice-weak threats like Dragonite, Garchomp, and Landorus-Therian.
* **Attributes:** Bypasses many defensive checks; notably, it is a non-contact move, avoiding effects like Rough Skin or Rocky Helmet.
