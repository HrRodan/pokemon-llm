## Identity & Mechanics
**Energy Ball** is a Special Grass-type move introduced in Generation IV.
* **Description:** The user focuses the latent power of the surrounding environment, condensing it into a vibrant sphere of natural energy before launching it at high speeds toward the target. It represents the quintessential specialization of nature-based energy manipulation.
* **Effect:** Inflicts regular damage to a single target.
* **Secondary Effect:** Has a 10% chance to lower the targetâ€™s Special Defense by one stage, potentially softening the opponent for subsequent Special attacks.
* **Targeting:** Single Target.

## Competitive Profile
* **Stats:** Power 90, Accuracy 100%, PP 10 (Max 16).
* **Role:** **Reliable STAB** / **Consistency Option**.
* **Usage:** Energy Ball serves as the standard "reliable" Special Grass-type attack. While **Giga Drain** (75 Power) is often preferred for its healing properties and **Leaf Storm** (130 Power) is used as a high-damage nuke, Energy Ball is the go-to choice for maximum consistent damage without the recoil of a stat drop. It is crucial for punching through bulky Water, Ground, and Rock types. It deals **Super Effective** damage to **Water, Ground, and Rock** types.
* **Attributes:** This move is a projectile; it is blocked by the Ability **Bulletproof**. It does not make contact.

## Distribution & Notable Users
It has a **very wide** distribution (297 learners), making it one of the most accessible Grass-type moves in the game.
* **Prominent Users:** Iconic STAB users include **Venusaur**, **Sceptile**, **Serperior**, **Roserade**, and **Shaymin**. It is also a staple on **Whimsicott** and **Lilligant**.
* **Coverage Utility:** Frequently utilized as a vital coverage move by non-Grass Special Attackers to bypass checks like Gastrodon, Swampert, or Great Tusk. Notable coverage users include **Alakazam**, **Gengar**, **Chandelure**, and **Flutter Mane**.
* **Acquisition:** Highly accessible, appearing as a **TM** (Technical Machine) in every generation since its debut in the Sinnoh region.
