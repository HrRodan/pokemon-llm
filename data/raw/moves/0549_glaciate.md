## Identity & Mechanics
**Glaciate** is a Special Ice-type move introduced in Generation V (Unova).
* **Description:** The user exhales a blast of absolute-zero air, freezing the atmosphere around the battlefield. This sub-zero gale coats enemies in a layer of restrictive frost, numbing their limbs and chilling their resolve to significantly hinder their mobility.
* **Effect:** Deals damage to all adjacent opponents and lowers each target's Speed stat by one stage.
* **Secondary Effect:** 100% chance to lower Speed (Guaranteed unless the target has an ability like Clear Body or is behind a Substitute).
* **Targeting:** All opponents (Spread move).

## Competitive Profile
* **Stats:** Power 65, Accuracy 95%, PP 10.
* **Role:** Speed Control / Spread Utility.
* **Usage:** Glaciate functions as the signature, upgraded version of *Icy Wind*. While its base power is relatively low for a signature move, its value lies in its **guaranteed Speed drop** across multiple targets. In double battles (VGC), it serves as a powerful utility tool to disrupt the opponent's turn order. It deals **Super Effective** damage to **Dragon, Flying, Grass, and Ground** types.
* **Attributes:** This is a non-contact, spread-damage move. It is particularly effective at breaking Focus Sashes while simultaneously providing team-wide momentum.
