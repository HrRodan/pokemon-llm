## Identity & Mechanics
**Ice Punch** is a Physical Ice-type move introduced in Generation I.
* **Description:** The user delivers a freezing blow with a fist coated in sub-zero energy. This chilling strike can flash-freeze the moisture in the air upon impact, potentially encasing the target in solid ice.
* **Effect:** Inflicts standard physical damage.
* **Secondary Effect:** 10% chance to **Freeze** the target.
* **Targeting:** Single Target.

## Competitive Profile
* **Stats:** Power 75, Accuracy 100%, PP 15 (Max 24).
* **Role:** Consistency Option / Physical Coverage.
* **Usage:** While it lacks the raw power of *Icicle Crash* (85 BP), Ice Punch is valued for its perfect accuracy and significantly wider distribution. It serves as a vital tool for physical attackers to hit 4x Ice-weak targets. It deals **Super Effective** damage to **Dragon, Flying, Grass,** and **Ground** types.
* **Attributes:** This is a **Contact** move and is categorized as a **Punching** move, meaning its power is increased by 20% when used by Pokémon with the **Iron Fist** ability.

## Distribution & Notable Users
It has a **Wide** distribution (227 learners), making it one of the most accessible physical coverage moves in the game.
* **Prominent Users:** Iconic users include **Weavile**, **Galarian Darmanitan**, and **Beartic**, who utilize it for reliable STAB (Same Type Attack Bonus). It is also a staple for **Hitmonchan** and **Iron Hands** due to ability and stat synergies.
* **Coverage Utility:** Frequently seen on non-Ice Pokémon such as **Machamp**, **Dragonite**, **Metagross**, and **Tyranitar** to surprise and OHKO (One-Shot Knock Out) threatening Dragon or Ground-type walls like Landorus-T or Garchomp.
* **Acquisition:** Historically significant as a Move Tutor staple and widely available via **TM/TR** in almost every generation, including the latest titles.
