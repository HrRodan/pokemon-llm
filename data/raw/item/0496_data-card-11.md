## Identity & Utility
**Data Card 11** is a **Data Card** introduced in **Generation IV** (specifically appearing in *Pokémon HeartGold and SoulSilver*).
* **Description:** A specialized record-keeping tool used to track personal achievements within the Pokéathlon. It specifically displays the cumulative number of points a trainer’s Pokémon have scored across various athletic competitions. It is a **Key Item-style tracker** that **cannot be bought** with standard Poké Dollars.
* **Cost:** 0 Poké Dollars (**Special Acquisition**; obtained via Athlete Points).

## Effect & Mechanics
* **Primary Effect:** Serves as a data module for the player's Pokéathlon records. When accessed, it displays the total points earned by the trainer during their participation in the Pokéathlon Dome activities.
* **Battle Attributes:**
    * **Fling Power:** N/A (This item cannot be held or used in a battle sequence).
    * **Competitive Use:** **None.** This is a flavor and completionist item used exclusively for the Pokéathlon mini-game sidequest.
* **Evolution/Interaction:** Does not evolve any Pokémon. It interacts solely with the Pokéathlon terminal system in the Johto region.

## Acquisition & Lore
* **Availability:** This item is acquired at the **Pokéathlon Dome** (located north of Goldenrod City). It is typically purchased from the Athlete Shop using **Athlete Points (AP)**, the specific currency earned by competing in Pokéathlon events, rather than standard currency.
* **Wild Held Item:** Not found on any wild Pokémon. This is a unique data-storage item tied specifically to the trainer's progress.
