## Identity & Utility
**Data Card 09** is a **Data Card** introduced in **Generation IV** (specifically appearing in the *HeartGold and SoulSilver* versions).
* **Description:** This is a specialized record-keeping tool used exclusively for the Pokéathlon, a series of athletic competitions for Pokémon. It is a **Key Item** that cannot be sold, traded, or consumed. It functions as a permanent log for a specific mini-game's performance.
* **Cost:** 0 Poké Dollars (Not for sale; acquired via special exchange).

## Effect & Mechanics
* **Primary Effect:** Records and displays the cumulative number of times the Trainer has achieved a first-place finish in the **Pokéathlon Disc Catch** event. 
* **Battle Attributes:**
    * **Fling Power:** N/A (Key Items cannot be used with the move Fling).
    * **Competitive Use:** None. This item exists solely for side-activity progress tracking and has no impact on traditional Pokémon battles or stats.
* **Evolution/Interaction:** This item does not facilitate evolution nor does it interact with any Pokémon abilities. It serves as a "completionist" trophy within the Pokéathlon Dome.

## Acquisition & Lore
* **Availability:** In *Pokémon HeartGold and SoulSilver*, this item is obtained at the **Pokéathlon Dome** (located north of Goldenrod City). It is typically purchased from the Athlete Shop using **Athlete Points (AP)** earned by participating in Pokéathlon events.
* **Wild Held Item:** This item is never held by wild Pokémon. It is a unique human-made electronic card designed for the Johto region's sporting events.
* **World Knowledge:** The Disc Catch is a "Skill" and "Jump" category event where the player must leap to catch flying discs. Data Card 09 allows players to track their mastery of this specific mini-game as they aim for the "Medalist" status or higher rankings within the Pokéathlon records.
