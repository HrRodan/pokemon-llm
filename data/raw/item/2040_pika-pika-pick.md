## Identity & Utility
**Pika-Pika Pick** is a **Picnic** item introduced in **Generation IX (Pokémon Scarlet & Violet)**.
* **Description:** A decorative, single-use consumable used specifically during the sandwich-making mini-game at Picnics. It features a distinct Pikachu-themed aesthetic. It is a consumable item that can be purchased in bulk.
* **Cost:** 480 Poké Dollars (Cheap/Accessible).

## Effect & Mechanics
* **Primary Effect:** Serves as the final component in sandwich construction. While primarily cosmetic, it functions to stabilize the ingredients on the sandwich. Successfully completing a sandwich provides the player with "Meal Powers," which can affect Pokémon catch rates, experience gain, encounter rates, and Shiny Pokémon odds.
* **Battle Attributes:**
    * **Fling Power:** N/A (This item cannot be held in battle and has no Fling effect).
    * **Competitive Use:** **None.** This item has no application in VGC or Smogon formats as it cannot be held or used in combat. Its utility is strictly limited to the "overworld" picnic mechanic.
* **Evolution/Interaction:** This item does not trigger evolution. It is consumed immediately upon the completion of a sandwich.

## Acquisition & Lore
* **Availability:** Widely available at **Every Wich Way** specialty shops located in major Paldean cities, including Mesagoza, Cascarrafa, and Levincia. 
* **Wild Held Item:** This item is never found held by wild Pokémon and cannot be acquired through the **Pickup** ability. It is a mass-produced commercial good within the Paldea region.
