## Identity & Utility
**Coupon 1** is a **Plot-Advancement (Key Item)** introduced in **Generation IV (Sinnoh)**.
* **Description:** It is the first of three promotional vouchers required to obtain a Pokémon Watch (Pokétch). As a Key Item, it is stored in a dedicated pocket, cannot be discarded, and is not consumed upon use; rather, the set is exchanged for the device.
* **Cost:** 0 Poké Dollars (Not for sale; unique Quest Item).

## Effect & Mechanics
* **Primary Effect:** When held in conjunction with Coupon 2 and Coupon 3, the player can exchange the set with the President of the Pokétch Company in Jubilife City to receive the Pokétch.
* **Battle Attributes:**
    * **Fling Power:** N/A (Key Items cannot be held or used in battle).
    * **Competitive Use:** None. This item exists solely for early-game progression within the Sinnoh region storyline.
* **Evolution/Interaction:** This item does not trigger Pokémon evolution. It serves as a mechanical gate for the "Pokétch" feature, which provides various utility apps (Clock, Calculator, Dowsing Machine, etc.).

## Acquisition & Lore
* **Availability:** Obtained in **Jubilife City**. Players must find three Clowns scattered throughout the city and answer a simple Pokémon-related trivia question correctly for each. **Coupon 1** is awarded by the first Clown (usually found near the Pokémon Center or the Poké Mart).
* **Wild Held Item:** This item is never held by wild Pokémon. It is a unique quest item provided by an NPC.
* **Context:** This quest serves as a tutorial for the player to explore Jubilife City and introduces the Pokétch, a central mechanic of the *Diamond, Pearl, and Platinum* (and *Brilliant Diamond/Shining Pearl*) experience.
