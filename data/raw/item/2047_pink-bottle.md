## Identity & Utility
**Pink-bottle** is a **Picnic** item introduced in **Generation IX (Pokémon Scarlet & Violet)**.
* **Description:** A cosmetic customization item used to change the visual appearance of the picnic table setup. It is a **Consumable/Static Decor** item that can be purchased and used during the Picnic feature. 
* **Cost:** 2000 Poké Dollars (**Moderate/Mid-range**).

## Effect & Mechanics
* **Primary Effect:** When participating in a Picnic, the player can swap the standard condiments/bottles for this "Pink Bottle" to change the aesthetic theme of the table. It has no mechanical impact on the Sandwich-making process or the resulting Meal Powers.
* **Battle Attributes:**
    * **Fling Power:** N/A (Cannot be used in battle).
    * **Competitive Use:** **None.** This is a purely cosmetic item for the Paldean overworld picnic mechanic and has no application in VGC or Smogon formats.
* **Evolution/Interaction:** This item does not trigger evolution or interact with Pokémon abilities. It is tied strictly to the player's picnic UI/customization options.

## Acquisition & Lore
* **Availability:** Commonly sold at **Picnic-Knacks** stores throughout the Paldea region, specifically in major hubs like **Mesagoza, Cascarrafa, and Levincia**. 
* **Wild Held Item:** This item is never held by wild Pokémon. It must be purchased through retail outlets specializing in outdoor gear and picnic supplies.
