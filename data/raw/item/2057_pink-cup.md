## Identity & Utility
**Pink Cup** is a **Picnic Item** introduced in **Generation IX (Pokémon Scarlet & Violet)**.
* **Description:** A decorative accessory used to customize the player's picnic set. It is a permanent cosmetic enhancement that changes the appearance of the tableware used during picnics. It is categorized as a standard consumable purchase that unlocks a visual style.
* **Cost:** 1500 Poké Dollars (Mid-range/Standard for picnic accessories).

## Effect & Mechanics
* **Primary Effect:** When a picnic is started, if the player has selected the Pink Cup set, the cups on the table will appear in a pink color scheme. This effect is purely aesthetic and provides no mechanical bonuses to sandwich making or egg discovery rates.
* **Battle Attributes:**
    * **Fling Power:** N/A (This item cannot be held by a Pokémon and cannot be used in battle).
    * **Competitive Use:** None. This item is strictly for overworld roleplay and customization within the Paldea region's picnic mechanic.
* **Evolution/Interaction:** Does not trigger evolution. It does not interact with abilities like Pickup or Klutz as it remains in the "Other Items" or "Picnic" pocket of the Bag and cannot be held.

## Acquisition & Lore
* **Availability:** Available for purchase at **Picnic-Knacks** specialty stores found in major Paldean hubs, such as **Mesagoza**, **Cascarrafa**, and **Levincia**.
* **Wild Held Item:** This item is never found on wild Pokémon. It is a manufactured commodity intended for trainers exploring the Paldea region.
