## Identity & Utility
**Mudbray Mud** is a **TM Material** introduced in **Generation IX** (Pokémon Scarlet & Violet).
* **Description:** A specialized crafting material representing the mud shed or collected from the Mudbray evolutionary line. It is a **Consumable Crafting Ingredient** that cannot be used directly in battle or on a Pokémon, but is essential for the Technical Machine Machine (TM Machine) system.
* **Cost:** 80 Poké Dollars (Cheap; primarily has value when exchanged for League Points (LP) or used in crafting).

## Effect & Mechanics
* **Primary Effect:** Used as a reagent to craft specific Technical Machines (TMs). It serves as a localized currency/component alongside League Points.
* **Battle Attributes:**
    * **Fling Power:** N/A (Cannot be held in battle).
    * **Competitive Use:** **None.** This item exists solely within the overworld economy and crafting menus. It provides no tactical advantage in competitive VGC or Smogon formats.
* **Evolution/Interaction:** This item is required to craft several Ground-type and physical moves. Using World Knowledge, notable TMs requiring Mudbray Mud include:
    * **TM028 (Stomp)**
    * **TM035 (Mud Shot)**
    * **TM051 (Sandstorm)**
    * **TM084 (Stomping Tantrum)**

## Acquisition & Lore
* **Availability:** Exclusively obtained in the **Paldea** region and other Gen IX locales. It is not purchased from Marts but is gathered through Pokémon battles.
* **Wild Held Item:** While not a traditional held item, it is dropped as loot after defeating or catching wild **Mudbray** or **Mudsdale**. It can also occasionally be found as a sparkling pickup on the ground in areas where these Pokémon reside, such as the South Province or West Province of Paldea.
