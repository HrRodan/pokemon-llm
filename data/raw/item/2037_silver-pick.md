## Identity & Utility
**Silver Pick** is a **Picnic** accessory introduced in **Generation IX (Paldea)**.
* **Description:** A silver-colored pick used during the sandwich-making mini-game at picnics. It serves as the final structural element to secure ingredients. It is a **Consumable** item that **Can** be bought in bulk. 
* **Cost:** 40 Poké Dollars (**Cheap/Accessible**).

## Effect & Mechanics
* **Primary Effect:** When making a sandwich at a picnic, the Silver Pick is used to pierce the bread and ingredients, finalizing the sandwich. While mostly cosmetic compared to basic picks, it is part of the progression of sandwich-making aesthetics.
* **Battle Attributes:**
    * **Fling Power:** N/A (Cannot be used in battle).
    * **Competitive Use:** **None.** Its utility is strictly limited to the overworld "Picnic" mechanic for triggering Meal Powers (such as Sparkling Power, Egg Power, or Encounter Power).
* **Evolution/Interaction:** Does not evolve any Pokémon. It is consumed upon the successful completion of a sandwich.

## Acquisition & Lore
* **Availability:** Commonly sold at specialized food and supply shops throughout the Paldea region, such as **Artisan Bakery** and **Aquiesta Supermarket**.
* **Wild Held Item:** Not found on wild Pokémon. It is a mass-produced culinary tool designed for trainers focusing on the sandwich-crafting mechanics of the Paldean wilderness.
