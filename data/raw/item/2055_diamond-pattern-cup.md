## Identity & Utility
**Diamond-Pattern Cup** is a **Picnic Accessory** introduced in **Generation IX (Pokémon Scarlet & Violet)**.
* **Description:** A cosmetic customization item used to personalize the player's picnic set. It is a non-battle, functional decorative item used specifically during the Picnic mechanic to change the visual theme of the table.
* **Cost:** 800 Poké Dollars (Cheap/Accessible).

## Effect & Mechanics
* **Primary Effect:** When selected in the Picnic decoration menu, it changes the appearance of the cups on the picnic table to a specific diamond-patterned design. It provides no mechanical bonuses to Meal Powers or Egg Power.
* **Battle Attributes:**
    * **Fling Power:** N/A (Cannot be held by a Pokémon in battle).
    * **Competitive Use:** None. This item is purely for the overworld "Picnic" aesthetic and has no impact on competitive VGC or Smogon formats.
* **Evolution/Interaction:** Does not trigger evolution. It is categorized under "Picnic" items, which are distinct from held items or medicines.

## Acquisition & Lore
* **Availability:** Commonly purchased at **Picnic Knacks** specialty stores located in major Paldean hubs such as **Mesagoza**, **Cascarrafa**, and **Levincia**. 
* **Wild Held Item:** This item is never found on wild Pokémon. It is a retail-exclusive item designed for trainer customization.
* **World Knowledge:** As part of the Generation IX Picnic system, this item represents the shift toward player-expression and "cozy" gameplay elements, allowing players to coordinate their picnic gear with their outfits or table cloths.
