## Identity & Utility
**Data Card 20** is a **Data Card** introduced in **Generation IV** (specifically appearing in *Pokémon HeartGold and SoulSilver*).
* **Description:** It is a specialized record-keeping tool used within the Pokéathlon Dome. It functions as a tracker for multiplayer statistics, specifically monitoring how many times a player has finished in last place during wireless competitions. It is a **Key-style Utility Item** that **cannot be bought** with standard currency.
* **Cost:** 0 Poké Dollars (**Not for sale**). It is acquired via Athlete Points (AP) rather than traditional money.

## Effect & Mechanics
* **Primary Effect:** Automatically records and displays the number of times the trainer has placed last overall in the Pokéathlon when competing against other players over a wireless connection.
* **Battle Attributes:**
    * **Fling Power:** N/A (This item cannot be held or used in battle).
    * **Competitive Use:** **None.** Its utility is strictly limited to the Pokéathlon mini-game records and has no impact on Smogon, VGC, or standard trainer battles.
* **Evolution/Interaction:** This item does not trigger evolution. It is part of a collectible set of 27 Data Cards used to populate the "Data" section of the Pokéathlon menu, providing a "completionist" element to the Johto region's side-activities.

## Acquisition & Lore
* **Availability:** Purchased at the **Pokéathlon Dome** (located north of Goldenrod City) at the Athlete Shop. These cards are typically unlocked and purchased using **Athlete Points (AP)** earned by participating in various Pokéathlon events.
* **Wild Held Item:** This item is never found on wild Pokémon and cannot be obtained through the **Pickup** ability. It is a unique prize/utility item designated for the player's personal records.
