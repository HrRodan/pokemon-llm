## Identity & Utility
**Green Petal** is a **Key Item (Quest Component)** introduced in **Generation VII (Alola)**.
* **Description:** A pressed flower petal received from the Trial Captain Mallow. It serves as a vital quest objective during Mina’s trial in the Alola region. It is a **Key Item** that **cannot** be bought or sold.
* **Cost:** 0 Poké Dollars (**Special Acquisition**; unique story item).

## Effect & Mechanics
* **Primary Effect:** The Green Petal has no direct effect in battle or on Pokémon stats. Its sole purpose is to serve as one of the seven colored petals required to complete the "Rainbow Flower" during Mina's trial in *Pokémon Ultra Sun* and *Ultra Moon*.
* **Battle Attributes:**
    * **Fling Power:** N/A (Key Items cannot be held or used with Fling).
    * **Competitive Use:** **None.** This item exists exclusively for story progression and trial completion within the single-player campaign.
* **Evolution/Interaction:** This item does not trigger evolution or interact with any Pokémon abilities. It is purely a narrative and quest-related tool.

## Acquisition & Lore
* **Availability:** Obtained specifically during the endgame trial of Captain Mina. The player must travel to **Lush Jungle** and defeat or interact with **Trial Captain Mallow** to receive this petal.
* **Wild Held Item:** This item is never found on wild Pokémon. It is a unique gift given by an NPC to signify the player's progress in the Island Challenge. 
* **Lore Context:** The collection of the Green Petal, along with six others (Red, Orange, Yellow, Blue, Pink, and Purple), is a requirement to challenge the Totem Pokémon at the end of Mina's trial, symbolizing the unity of the Trial Captains across Alola.
