## Identity & Utility
**Purple Petal** is a **Quest/Key Item** introduced in **Generation VII** (Pokémon Ultra Sun & Ultra Moon).
* **Description:** A pressed flower petal received from the Kahuna Nanu during Mina’s Trial on Poni Island. It is a non-consumable **Key Item** that cannot be bought, sold, or held by Pokémon. It serves as a vital components of the "Rainbow Flower" required to complete the Alola Island Challenge.
* **Cost:** 0 Poké Dollars (**Not for sale**).

## Effect & Mechanics
* **Primary Effect:** Acts as a progression marker for Mina's Trial in *Pokémon Ultra Sun* and *Ultra Moon*. The player must collect seven different colored petals (Red, Orange, Yellow, Green, Blue, Pink, and Purple) from various Captains and Kahunas across the Alola region to proceed to the battle against the Totem Pokémon, Ribombee.
* **Battle Attributes:**
    * **Fling Power:** N/A (Key Items cannot be Flung in battle).
    * **Competitive Use:** **None.** This item exists solely for story progression and trial completion within the single-player campaign.
* **Evolution/Interaction:** While it does not evolve Pokémon, it is a required trigger to eventually encounter and battle **Totem Ribombee**. It has no interaction with abilities like Pickup or Klutz as it cannot be held.

## Acquisition & Lore
* **Availability:** In *Pokémon Ultra Sun* and *Pokémon Ultra Moon*, this specific petal is obtained by defeating or interacting with **Nanu** at the Aether House on Ula'ula Island during the final stages of Mina’s Trial.
* **Wild Held Item:** This item is never found on wild Pokémon; it is a unique quest item tied to the Trial Captain Mina and the Kahuna Nanu.
* **Lore Context:** The Purple Petal represents one of the many bonds the player has formed with the trial captains across the islands. Once all seven petals are gathered, they are woven into the **Rainbow Flower**, signaling the player's readiness for the final trial of the Poni Island challenge.
