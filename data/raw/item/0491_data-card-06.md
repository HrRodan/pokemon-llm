## Identity & Utility
**Data Card 06** is a **Data Card** introduced in **Generation IV** (specifically appearing in the *HeartGold and SoulSilver* Johto expansions).
* **Description:** It is a specialized record-keeping item used to track performance metrics within the Pokéathlon. This specific card acts as a ledger for the **Relay Run** event. It is a **Key-adjacent utility item** that cannot be bought with standard currency or sold.
* **Cost:** 0 Poké Dollars (**Not for sale**; acquired via specialized in-game points).

## Effect & Mechanics
* **Primary Effect:** Records and displays the cumulative number of times the player has achieved **first place** in the **Pokéathlon Relay Run**. It serves as a permanent stat-tracker for that specific mini-game.
* **Battle Attributes:**
    * **Fling Power:** N/A (This item cannot be held or used in battle).
    * **Competitive Use:** **None.** This is a completionist/side-quest item with no impact on Pokémon stats, moves, or competitive battling (VGC/Smogon).
* **Evolution/Interaction:** Does not evolve any Pokémon or interact with battle abilities. It functions strictly as a data-logging tool within the Pokéathlon Dome interface.

## Acquisition & Lore
* **Availability:** This item is obtained at the **Pokéathlon Dome** (located north of Goldenrod City in Johto). It is typically purchased from the Athlete Shop using **Athlete Points (AP)** earned by participating in various Pokéathlon challenges.
* **Wild Held Item:** This item is never found on wild Pokémon. It is a unique reward for participating in the Johto region's athletic competitions.
* **Context:** Data Cards are essential for players aiming to complete the "Pokéathlon Medalist" goals, providing a way to verify high scores and milestone achievements across different event categories.
