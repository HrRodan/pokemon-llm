## Identity & Utility
**Gastly Gas** is a **TM Material** introduced in **Generation IX (Pokémon Scarlet & Violet)**.
* **Description:** A specific crafting resource dropped by members of the Gastly evolutionary line. It is a **Consumable Crafting Material** used at TM Machines. While it has a technical buy/sell value, it cannot be purchased from standard Poke Marts.
* **Cost:** 80 Poké Dollars (Cheap/Resource).

## Effect & Mechanics
* **Primary Effect:** This item serves as a fundamental ingredient for crafting Technical Machines (TMs) at the TM Machine found in Pokémon Centers. Players combine Gastly Gas with League Points (LP) to create specific moves.
* **Battle Attributes:**
    * **Fling Power:** N/A (Cannot be held in battle).
    * **Competitive Use:** None. This is a progression and customization item used exclusively in the overworld menu or at crafting stations.
* **Evolution/Interaction:** Does not trigger evolution. It is a modern mechanic item categorized under "TM Materials," a system designed to encourage players to battle wild Pokémon to "learn" new moves.

## Acquisition & Lore
* **Availability:** Obtained as a guaranteed or high-probability drop after defeating or catching wild Pokémon in the Gastly family. It can also be exchanged for League Points (LP).
* **Wild Held Item:** Dropped by wild **Gastly**, **Haunter**, and **Gengar**. In the Paldea region, players often farm this item during Ghost-type Mass Outbreaks or by exploring ruins and dark areas where these Pokémon congregate at night.
