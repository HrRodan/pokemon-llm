## Identity & Utility
**Data Card 25** is a **Data Card** introduced in **Generation IV** (specifically appearing in *Pokémon HeartGold and SoulSilver*).
* **Description:** This item serves as a specialized record-keeping tool for the **Pokéathlon**, a series of athletic mini-games. It is a **Key Item-style utility** that displays a specific statistic: the number of times a trainer’s Pokémon have won individual prizes (medals/honors) during events. It is a non-consumable, non-holdable data log.
* **Cost:** 0 Poké Dollars (**Special Acquisition**). It cannot be bought with standard currency; it is typically exchanged for Athlete Points (AP) at the Pokéathlon Dome.

## Effect & Mechanics
* **Primary Effect:** When possessed, it unlocks or records the specific metric of "Individual Prizes" earned by the trainer's Pokémon. This data can be viewed at the Pokéathlon Dome's information terminals.
* **Battle Attributes:**
    * **Fling Power:** N/A (This item cannot be held or used in battle).
    * **Competitive Use:** **None.** This item has no impact on competitive battling, IVs, EVs, or move sets. It is strictly for flavor, completionism, and tracking mini-game achievements.
* **Evolution/Interaction:** This item does not facilitate evolution and does not interact with any Pokémon abilities (such as Pickup or Klutz).

## Acquisition & Lore
* **Availability:** In *Pokémon HeartGold and SoulSilver*, Data Cards are purchased from the **Athlete Shop** located within the Pokéathlon Dome (north of Goldenrod City). They are bought using **Athlete Points (AP)** earned by participating in Pokéathlon hurdles, relay runs, and other events.
* **Wild Held Item:** None. This item is never found on wild Pokémon and is unique to the trainer’s progress.
* **World Knowledge Context:** Data Card 25 is one of 27 collectible cards. These cards are essential for players aiming for 100% completion within the Pokéathlon records, as they provide the only way to track granular historical data for the trainer's roster across the various athletic courses (Speed, Power, Skill, Stamina, and Jump).
