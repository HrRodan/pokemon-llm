## Identity & Utility
**Data Card 01** is a **Data Card** introduced in **Generation IV** (specifically within the Johto-region-based titles *HeartGold and SoulSilver*).
* **Description:** A specialized digital record-keeping device used to track specific performance metrics within the Pokéathlon. It is a **Key Item variant** that is kept in the player's Bag and cannot be used, held, or consumed in battle. 
* **Cost:** 0 Poké Dollars (**Not for sale**; acquired via special currency).

## Effect & Mechanics
* **Primary Effect:** Records and displays the total number of times the Trainer has secured **first place overall** in the Pokéathlon competitions. It serves as a permanent achievement log for the player's success in the Pokéathlon Dome.
* **Battle Attributes:**
    * **Fling Power:** N/A (Key Items cannot be thrown using Fling).
    * **Competitive Use:** **None.** This item has no utility in VGC, Smogon, or standard trainer battles. It is strictly a record-tracking tool for a side-activity.
* **Evolution/Interaction:** This card is part of a series of 27 collectible Data Cards. It interacts solely with the Pokéathlon interface and does not affect Pokémon evolution, breeding, or combat abilities.

## Acquisition & Lore
* **Availability:** Found exclusively at the **Pokéathlon Dome**. It is typically purchased from the Athlete Shop using **Athlete Points (AP)** earned by participating in Pokéathlon events, rather than standard currency.
* **Wild Held Item:** This item is never held by wild Pokémon. It cannot be gathered via the **Pickup** ability or stolen using Thief/Covet, as it is a unique data-logging tool.
