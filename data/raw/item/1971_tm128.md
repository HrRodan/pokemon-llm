## Identity & Utility
**TM128 (Amnesia)** is a **Technical Machine** introduced in **Generation IX (Pokémon Scarlet & Violet)**.
* **Description:** This item provides a way to teach the move **Amnesia** to compatible Pokémon. As part of the modern TM system in Paldea, it is a single-use consumable that can be crafted repeatedly at any TM Machine once the recipe is unlocked.
* **Cost:** 0 Poké Dollars (Not for sale at standard shops). It is primarily acquired through crafting using **800 LP (League Points)** and specific Pokémon materials.

## Effect & Mechanics
* **Primary Effect:** Teaches the move **Amnesia**. When used in battle, Amnesia sharply raises the user's **Special Defense** stat by two stages (+2).
* **Battle Attributes:**
    * **Fling Power:** N/A (Technical Machines cannot be used with the move Fling).
    * **Competitive Use:** **Niche Defensive Setup.** While not a universal staple, Amnesia is used on specific "wall" Pokémon or setup sweepers to bolster their survivability against Special Attackers. In formats like VGC or Smogon, it is often seen on bulky Pokémon like **Dondozo** or **Slowbro** to mitigate damage from high-tier threats like Flutter Mane or Gholdengo.
* **Evolution/Interaction:** This item does not trigger evolution. It is a data-disc used for move-learning and is consumed upon use.

## Acquisition & Lore
* **Availability:** 
    * **Crafting Recipe:** In *Scarlet & Violet*, the recipe for TM128 is typically unlocked after defeating a specific Team Star boss or finding the TM in the overworld.
    * **Materials Required:** To craft this TM, the player must provide **3x Slowpoke Claw** and **3x Slakoth Fur**.
* **Wild Held Item:** This item is never held by wild Pokémon. It is a manufactured technological item found in Poké Balls scattered across the **Paldea region** or the **Terarium (Indigo Disk)**.
