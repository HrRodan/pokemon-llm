## Identity & Utility
**Devon Goods** is a **Plot Advancement** item introduced in **Generation III (Hoenn)**.
* **Description:** A package containing high-tech mechanical parts manufactured by the Devon Corporation. As a **Key Item**, it is essential for storyline progression and cannot be used in battle, sold, or given to a Pokémon to hold.
* **Cost:** 0 Poké Dollars (Not for sale / Key Item).

## Effect & Mechanics
* **Primary Effect:** Serves as a critical quest objective. The player must deliver these parts to **Captain Stern** in Slateport City to advance the narrative.
* **Battle Attributes:**
    * **Fling Power:** N/A (Key Items cannot be held or used with the move Fling).
    * **Competitive Use:** None. This item exists solely for the single-player campaign/story mode and has no mechanical impact on competitive Pokémon battles.
* **Evolution/Interaction:** This item does not trigger evolution or interact with abilities like Pickup or Klutz. It is strictly a narrative tool.

## Acquisition & Lore
* **Availability:** Obtained in **Rustboro City** from a Devon Corporation researcher after the player retrieves the stolen goods from a Team Aqua or Team Magma Grunt in the **Rusturf Tunnel**. 
* **Wild Held Item:** This item is never held by wild Pokémon. It is a unique, one-of-a-kind item provided by an NPC.
* **Lore Context:** The Devon Goods represent the technological rivalry and industrial themes of the Hoenn region. Their delivery is the primary motivation for the player's first journey across the sea to Dewford Town and Slateport City, facilitating the first meeting with Mr. Briney and Steven Stone.
