## Identity & Utility
**Dynamax Crystal (sgr7194)** is a **Special Event Item** (Categorized as a Dynamax Crystal) introduced in **Generation VIII** (Galar region).
* **Description:** This is a single-use consumable item used specifically in *Pokémon Sword & Shield*. When taken to the Watchtower Lair in the Wild Area, it triggers a unique Max Raid Battle. It is a **consumable event item** that cannot be purchased through standard gameplay.
* **Cost:** 20 Poké Dollars (**Special Acquisition**; while it has a nominal value in the data, it is not sold in Poké Marts and is typically obtained via serial codes or promotions).

## Effect & Mechanics
* **Primary Effect:** Activating this crystal at the Watchtower Lair causes a specific Pokémon to appear as a Dynamax Raid boss. The encounter remains active until the end of the day (midnight) or until the Pokémon is defeated.
* **Battle Attributes:**
    * **Fling Power:** N/A (This item cannot be held or used with the move Fling).
    * **Competitive Use:** **Niche/Indirect.** While the item itself has no utility in a competitive battle, Dynamax Crystals were frequently used to distribute Pokémon with **Hidden Abilities**, specific **Gigantamax** factors, or high IV yields, making them essential for breeding and training competitive-grade teams during the Sword & Shield era.
* **Evolution/Interaction:** Does not trigger evolution. It interacts exclusively with the **Watchtower Lair** environment in the Galar Wild Area.

## Acquisition & Lore
* **Availability:** This item is not available through standard NPC vendors or Marts. It was distributed via **Serial Codes** as part of specific retail promotions (such as those from GameStop, Target, or international equivalents) or pre-order bonuses. 
* **Wild Held Item:** Never found on wild Pokémon. It is an external event-based item that does not interact with the **Pickup** ability or wild encounter tables.
