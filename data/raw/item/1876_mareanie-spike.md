## Identity & Utility
**Mareanie Spike** is a **TM Material** introduced in **Generation IX (Pokémon Scarlet & Violet)**.
* **Description:** A specialized crafting material dropped by the Mareanie evolutionary line. It is a **Consumable Crafting Resource** used primarily at the Technical Machine (TM) Machine found at Pokémon Centers. While it can be sold, it cannot be bought from standard Poké Marts.
* **Cost:** 80 Poké Dollars (**Cheap/Resource Value**).

## Effect & Mechanics
* **Primary Effect:** Serves as a required currency/component for crafting specific Technical Machines (TMs). It has no direct use in the overworld or on a Pokémon in battle.
* **Battle Attributes:**
    * **Fling Power:** N/A (Cannot be held or flung in standard competitive play).
    * **Competitive Use:** **None.** This item exists purely for the Generation IX crafting economy. It is not a held item and provides no statistical or tactical advantage in battle.
* **Evolution/Interaction:** Does not trigger evolution. It is strictly utilized as a recipe ingredient for Poison-type and spike-themed moves.

## Acquisition & Lore
* **Availability:** Obtained as a drop after defeating or catching wild **Mareanie** or **Toxapex**. It is predominantly found in the coastal and beach biomes of the **Paldea** region.
* **Wild Held Item:** This item is not "held" in the traditional sense; rather, it is added directly to the "TM Materials" pocket of the bag upon the conclusion of an encounter with the Mareanie line.
* **Crafting Context:** In *Pokémon Scarlet & Violet*, Mareanie Spikes are essential for crafting high-value Poison-type TMs, most notably:
    * **TM091 (Toxic Spikes)**
    * **TM102 (Gunk Shot)**
    * **TM148 (Sludge Bomb)**
