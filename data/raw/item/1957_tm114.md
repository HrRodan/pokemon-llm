## Identity & Utility
**TM114** is a **Technical Machine (TM)** introduced in **Generation IX (Scarlet & Violet)**.
* **Description:** A single-use move-teaching device (in Generation IX) that enables a compatible Pokémon to learn the move **Shadow Ball**. It is categorized as a Machine and is a vital tool for customizing Pokémon movepools.
* **Cost:** 0 Poké Dollars (Not for sale at standard retail). In the Paldea region, it is acquired through exploration or by using the **TM Machine** located at Pokémon Centers.

## Effect & Mechanics
* **Primary Effect:** Teaches the move **Shadow Ball** to a compatible Pokémon. 
    * **Move Profile (Shadow Ball):** A Ghost-type Special move with 80 Base Power and 100% Accuracy. It has a 20% chance to lower the target's Special Defense by one stage.
* **Battle Attributes:**
    * **Fling Power:** N/A (Technical Machines cannot be held or used with the move Fling in modern generations).
    * **Competitive Use:** **High/Essential.** Shadow Ball is the gold standard for Special Ghost-type offense. It is a staple move for Ghost-types like Gengar, Flutter Mane, and Skeledirge, as well as a common coverage move for many Psychic and Fairy-type Special Attackers.
* **Evolution/Interaction:** This item does not trigger evolution. It is consumed upon use in Generation IX, though it can be crafted multiple times.

## Acquisition & Lore
* **Availability:** Found as a collectible item in the overworld throughout the Paldea region. Once obtained, it can be replicated at any TM Machine.
* **Crafting Requirements:** To craft TM114, the player typically requires:
    * **3,000 LP** (League Points)
    * **3x Gastly Gas**
    * **3x Sandygast Sand**
* **Wild Held Item:** Not found on wild Pokémon. TMs in this generation are primarily sourced through the crafting system using materials gathered from wild Pokémon encounters (specifically Ghost-types like Gastly and Sandygast for this move).
