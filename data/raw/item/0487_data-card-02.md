## Identity & Utility
**Data Card 02** is a **Data Card** introduced in **Generation IV** (specifically appearing in the Johto-based *HeartGold and SoulSilver* expansions).
* **Description:** It is a specialized record-keeping tool used to track personal performance metrics within the Pokéathlon. This card specifically logs the number of times the player has finished in last place. It is a **Key Item-style collectible** that cannot be bought with standard currency or sold.
* **Cost:** 0 Poké Dollars (**Not for sale**). It is typically acquired via the exchange of Athlete Points (AP) within the Pokéathlon Dome.

## Effect & Mechanics
* **Primary Effect:** Records and displays the total number of times the trainer has come in **last place overall** across all Pokéathlon events.
* **Battle Attributes:**
    * **Fling Power:** N/A (This item cannot be held or used in a standard Pokémon battle).
    * **Competitive Use:** **None.** This item exists solely for mini-game tracking and has no utility in VGC, Smogon, or standard trainer battles. It is a niche collectible for completionists and Pokéathlon enthusiasts.
* **Evolution/Interaction:** Does not trigger evolution. It functions as a data-log within the Pokéathlon's "Data Room," allowing players to track their progress (or lack thereof) toward specific medals and trophies.

## Acquisition & Lore
* **Availability:** Available exclusively at the **Pokéathlon Dome** (located north of Goldenrod City in Johto). It is purchased from the Athlete Shop using **Athlete Points (AP)**, typically available on specific days of the week.
* **Wild Held Item:** This item is never held by wild Pokémon. It is a unique human-made digital record.
* **Context:** Data Cards are part of a series of 27 cards that provide transparency into a player’s performance history. Data Card 02 is often viewed as a "shame" or "learning" metric, helping players identify which events or Pokémon teams are underperforming in the high-speed athletic competition.
