## Identity & Utility
**TM133 (Earth Power)** is a **Technical Machine** introduced in its current iteration in **Generation IX (Scarlet & Violet)**.
* **Description:** A data-encoding disc used to teach the move **Earth Power** to compatible Pokémon. In the Generation IX ecosystem, TMs are single-use consumables that can be replicated at a TM Machine using materials and League Points (LP). 
* **Cost:** 0 Poké Dollars (Not for sale at standard Marts; requires **10,000 LP** and specific materials to craft).

## Effect & Mechanics
* **Primary Effect:** Teaches the move **Earth Power**. 
    * **Move Profile:** A Special Ground-type attack with **90 Base Power** and **100% Accuracy**. It has a **10% chance** to lower the target's Special Defense stat by one stage.
* **Battle Attributes:**
    * **Fling Power:** N/A (TMs generally cannot be Flung for damage in Gen IX).
    * **Competitive Use:** **High-Tier Staple.** Earth Power is the gold standard for Special Ground-type coverage. It is vital for Special Attackers to threaten Steel, Fire, Rock, Poison, and Electric types. It is frequently seen on competitive staples such as **Glimmora**, **Landorus-Therian**, **Ursaluna-Bloodmoon**, and **Iron Moth**.
* **Evolution/Interaction:** This item does not trigger evolution. It interacts with the **TM Machine** mechanic found in the Paldea and Blueberry Academy regions.

## Acquisition & Lore
* **Availability:** Found as a static pickup in the overworld of **Paldea** (notably in **Area Zero** and the **Asado Desert**) or rewarded through League Rep milestones. In the **Indigo Disk** DLC, it is accessible via the TM Machine once the recipe is unlocked.
* **Crafting Requirements:** To replicate TM133, a trainer requires:
    * **10,000 LP**
    * **5x Barboach Slime** (Gathered from the Barboach line)
    * **3x Silicobra Sand** (Gathered from the Silicobra line)
    * **3x Shellos Mucus** (Gathered from the Shellos line)
* **Wild Held Item:** Not held by wild Pokémon; exclusively obtained via exploration or crafting.
