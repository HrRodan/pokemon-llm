## Identity & Utility
**Red Petal** is a **Key Item** (Quest Item) introduced in **Generation VII (Alola)**.
* **Description:** A pressed flower petal received from Trial Captain Kiawe. It serves as a necessary component for Mina’s trial in *Pokémon Ultra Sun* and *Pokémon Ultra Moon*. It is a **Key Item** that **cannot** be bought, sold, or held by Pokémon.
* **Cost:** 0 Poké Dollars (**Not for sale**).

## Effect & Mechanics
* **Primary Effect:** The Red Petal is a quest-critical item. It is one of seven colored petals (Red, Orange, Yellow, Green, Blue, Indigo, and Pink) that must be gathered from various Trial Captains across the Alola region.
* **Battle Attributes:**
    * **Fling Power:** N/A (Key Items cannot be used with the move Fling).
    * **Competitive Use:** **None.** This item exists solely for story progression and cannot be used in competitive formats or held in battle.
* **Evolution/Interaction:** Once all seven petals are collected, they are combined by Mina to create the **Rainbow Flower**, which triggers the final stage of the trial: the battle against the Totem Pokémon, **Ribombee**.

## Acquisition & Lore
* **Availability:** Exclusively obtained during the post-game/late-game trial of Mina in *Pokémon Ultra Sun* and *Pokémon Ultra Moon*.
* **Source:** Given to the player by **Trial Captain Kiawe** at Wela Volcano Park after the player defeats him (or his Alolan Marowak) in a challenge.
* **Wild Held Item:** None. This item is unique and cannot be found on wild Pokémon or gathered via the Pickup ability.
