## Identity & Utility
**Honor of Kalos** is a **Plot-Advancement Key Item** introduced in **Generation VI** (Pokémon X & Y).
* **Description:** It is a precious, symbolic trophy awarded to the player for their heroic actions and for achieving the title of Kalos Champion. As a Key Item, it is **Consumable only in a narrative sense** and **cannot be bought, sold, or held** by a Pokémon.
* **Cost:** 0 Poké Dollars (Not for sale / Unique Story Item).

## Effect & Mechanics
* **Primary Effect:** This item has **no functional effect** in battle, training, or overworld navigation. Its sole purpose is to serve as a permanent mark of prestige within the player's inventory, signifying the completion of the main Kalos story arc.
* **Battle Attributes:**
    * **Fling Power:** N/A (Key Items cannot be held or used with Fling).
    * **Competitive Use:** **None.** This item exists purely for flavor and plot progression; it provides no tactical advantage in VGC, Smogon, or in-game battles.
* **Evolution/Interaction:** Does not trigger evolution or interact with any Pokémon abilities (such as Pickup or Klutz). It remains in the Key Items pocket of the Bag indefinitely.

## Acquisition & Lore
* **Availability:** Awarded automatically during the post-game sequence in Pokémon X and Y. It is presented to the player by Professor Sycamore during the grand parade in Lumiose City after the player defeats the Elite Four, the Champion, and AZ. 
* **Wild Held Item:** This item is unique and cannot be found on wild Pokémon. It is a one-of-a-kind commemorative medal that represents the player's status as the "Honorary Hero" of the Kalos region.
