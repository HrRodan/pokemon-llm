## Identity & Utility
**Lens Case** is a **Gameplay / Key Item** introduced in **Generation VI (Kalos)**.
* **Description:** A chic-looking storage case designed to hold various colored contact lenses. It is a **Key Item** (Permanent/Non-consumable) that **cannot be bought**; it is typically gifted to the player as part of the character customization mechanics.
* **Cost:** 0 Poké Dollars (**Not for sale**).

## Effect & Mechanics
* **Primary Effect:** Grants the player the ability to change their eye color at specific locations, such as PR Video Studios or dressing rooms in Boutiques. This is a purely cosmetic feature used to personalize the player's avatar.
* **Battle Attributes:**
    * **Fling Power:** N/A (Key Items cannot be held or used with the move Fling).
    * **Competitive Use:** **None**. This item exists solely for overworld character customization and has no impact on battle stats, typing, or Pokémon performance.
* **Evolution/Interaction:** Does not interact with Pokémon evolution or abilities. It is tied exclusively to the player’s aesthetic appearance.

## Acquisition & Lore
* **Availability:** In its debut region (Kalos), it is obtained from a stylist in the **PR Video Studio** in Lumiose City. In Generation VII (Alola), it is provided to the player automatically to manage the expanded roster of contact lens colors available in apparel shops.
* **Wild Held Item:** This item is never held by wild Pokémon. It is a unique progression item tied to the player's inventory.
