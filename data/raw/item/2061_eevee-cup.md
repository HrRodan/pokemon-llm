## Identity & Utility
**Eevee Cup** is a **Picnic Item** introduced in **Generation IX (Pokémon Scarlet & Violet)**.
* **Description:** A decorative cup featuring an Eevee motif, used specifically to customize the player's picnic set during the Picnic mechanic. It is a **Reusable Cosmetic** item that is used to personalize the overworld environment while interacting with Pokémon or making sandwiches.
* **Cost:** 2000 Poké Dollars (**Mid-range/Affordable**).

## Effect & Mechanics
* **Primary Effect:** When a player initiates a **Picnic**, the Eevee Cup can be selected to change the visual appearance of the picnic table's accessories. It serves no functional purpose in HP restoration, status healing, or stat boosting; its value is purely aesthetic.
* **Battle Attributes:**
    * **Fling Power:** N/A (Cannot be held or used in battle).
    * **Competitive Use:** **None.** This item is strictly for overworld flavor and has no impact on competitive formats (VGC/Smogon) or battle performance.
* **Evolution/Interaction:** Does not trigger evolution or interact with battle abilities. It is part of the broader **Picnic Knacks** collection used for campsite customization.

## Acquisition & Lore
* **Availability:** Commonly purchased at **Picnic Knacks** specialty shops located in major Paldean cities such as **Mesagoza, Cascarrafa, and Levincia**. 
* **Wild Held Item:** This item is not held by wild Pokémon and cannot be obtained through the Pickup ability. It is acquired exclusively through retail purchase for player expression.
* **Lore Context:** As part of the Generation IX emphasis on "Treasure Hunt" and social interaction, the Eevee Cup represents the brand integration of popular Pokémon species into everyday lifestyle goods within the Pokémon world.
