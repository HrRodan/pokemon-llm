## Identity & Utility
**Data Card 16** is a **Data Card** (Key Item) introduced in **Generation IV** (specifically appearing in *Pokémon HeartGold and SoulSilver*).
* **Description:** A specialized digital record-keeping device used to track specific performance metrics within the Pokéathlon. This card focuses exclusively on the **Ring Drop** event. It is a **Key Item** that **cannot** be bought or sold.
* **Cost:** 0 Poké Dollars (Not for sale; acquired through gameplay rewards).

## Effect & Mechanics
* **Primary Effect:** Records and displays the number of times the player has achieved a first-place finish in the **Pokéathlon Ring Drop** event. 
* **Battle Attributes:**
    * **Fling Power:** N/A (This item cannot be held or used in battle; it resides in the Key Items pocket).
    * **Competitive Use:** **None.** This is a flavor and completionist item used strictly for tracking mini-game statistics in the Johto region's Pokéathlon Dome.
* **Evolution/Interaction:** Does not evolve any Pokémon or interact with battle abilities. It serves as a permanent record within the player's inventory.

## Acquisition & Lore
* **Availability:** Purchased at the **Pokéathlon Dome** (located north of Goldenrod City) in exchange for **500 Athlete Points (AP)**. It is typically available for purchase after the player has obtained the National Pokédex or reached specific milestones in the Pokéathlon.
* **Wild Held Item:** This item is never held by wild Pokémon. It is a unique, player-bound item intended to showcase the player's mastery of the "Power" and "Stamina" based Ring Drop event, where Pokémon attempt to knock opponents out of a ring.
