## Identity & Utility
**Data Card 05** is a **Data Card** introduced in **Generation IV** (specifically appearing in *Pokémon HeartGold and SoulSilver*).
* **Description:** It is a specialized record-keeping device used to track specific achievements within the Pokéathlon. This card specifically logs the number of first-place victories the player has achieved in the **Hurdle Dash** event. It is a **Key Item-style collectible** that cannot be bought with standard currency.
* **Cost:** 0 Poké Dollars (**Not for sale**; acquired via special points).

## Effect & Mechanics
* **Primary Effect:** Records and displays the player's historical performance in the Pokéathlon's Hurdle Dash. It serves as a permanent record of "1st place" finishes for that specific mini-game.
* **Battle Attributes:**
    * **Fling Power:** N/A (This item cannot be held or used in battle; it resides in the Key Items pocket).
    * **Competitive Use:** **None.** This is a completionist/flavor item intended for the Pokéathlon side-quest. It offers no tactical advantage in Trainer battles or VGC.
* **Evolution/Interaction:** This item has no interaction with Pokémon evolution, breeding, or combat abilities.

## Acquisition & Lore
* **Availability:** This item is obtained at the **Pokéathlon Dome** (located north of Goldenrod City in Johto). It is typically purchased from the **Athlete Shop** using **Athlete Points (AP)**, which are earned by participating in Pokéathlon events.
* **Wild Held Item:** This item is never found on wild Pokémon. It is a unique player-specific record card.
* **Context:** Data Cards were a series of 27 collectible items in *HeartGold and SoulSilver* that allowed players to track their progress across different events and categories, serving as a trophy room of sorts for the most dedicated Pokéathletes.
