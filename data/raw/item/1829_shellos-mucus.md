## Identity & Utility
**Shellos Mucus** is a **TM Material** introduced in **Generation IX**.
* **Description:** A specialized crafting resource dropped by the Shellos evolutionary line. It is a **Consumable** material used at the Technical Machine Machine (TM Machine) to create various TMs. It cannot be used directly on a Pokémon.
* **Cost:** 80 Poké Dollars (Cheap/Accessible; primarily valued for its League Point conversion rate or crafting utility).

## Effect & Mechanics
* **Primary Effect:** Used as a required ingredient for crafting specific Technical Machines (TMs) in the Paldea region. Players must combine a specific quantity of Shellos Mucus with League Points (LP) at a Pokémon Center.
* **Battle Attributes:**
    * **Fling Power:** N/A (This item cannot be held or used in battle).
    * **Competitive Use:** **Utility only.** While it has no impact during a match, it is essential for competitive preparation to craft vital moves for a team's moveset.
* **Evolution/Interaction:** Does not trigger evolution. It is strictly a crafting currency.

## Acquisition & Lore
* **Availability:** Obtained as a drop after defeating or catching wild members of the Shellos family. It can also be received as a reward from Tera Raid Battles involving these Pokémon.
* **Wild Held Item:** Collected from wild **Shellos** and **Gastrodon** (both West Sea and East Sea variants). In Generation IX mechanics, these materials are gathered automatically upon victory rather than being held as a traditional item.
