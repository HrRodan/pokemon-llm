## Identity & Utility
**Basement Key** is a **Plot-Advancement Key Item** introduced in **Generation II** (and featured prominently in Generation III).
* **Description:** A specialized key used to unlock restricted subterranean areas. As a Key Item, it resides in a separate pocket of the Bag and is intended for overworld use to bypass environmental gates. It cannot be consumed, traded, or discarded.
* **Cost:** 0 Poké Dollars (Not for sale; Unique acquisition).

## Effect & Mechanics
* **Primary Effect:** Unlocks specific doors leading to underground facilities. 
    * **In Johto (Gen II/IV):** Opens the locked door in the Goldenrod Tunnel, providing access to the Underground Warehouse during the Team Rocket takeover.
    * **In Hoenn (Gen III/VI):** Unlocks the gate to **New Mauville**, a power plant located beneath Mauville City.
* **Battle Attributes:**
    * **Fling Power:** N/A (Key Items cannot be used with the move Fling).
    * **Competitive Use:** None. This item has no utility in competitive Pokémon battles (VGC/Smogon) and is strictly used for story progression and exploration.
* **Evolution/Interaction:** While it does not trigger evolution directly, it grants access to areas where specific Electric-type Pokémon (like Magnemite and Voltorb) can be caught and where specific items (like the Thunderbolt TM or Thunder Stone) may be found.

## Acquisition & Lore
* **Availability:** 
    * **Hoenn Region:** Gifted to the player by **Gym Leader Wattson** in Mauville City. He requests the player's help in shutting down the generator in New Mauville to prevent safety hazards.
    * **Johto Region:** Handed to the player by the **Director of the Radio Tower** (or a decoy) during the infiltration of the Goldenrod Radio Tower to help defeat Team Rocket.
* **Wild Held Item:** This item is never held by wild Pokémon; it is a unique item typically obtained through NPC interaction to signify plot advancement.
