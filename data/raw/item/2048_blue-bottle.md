## Identity & Utility
**Blue Bottle** is a **Picnic** accessory introduced in **Generation IX (Paldea)**.
* **Description:** The Blue Bottle is a decorative item used specifically for customizing the aesthetic appearance of the player's picnic table. It is a **Consumable/Decorative** item that **Can** be bought at specialized retail outlets.
* **Cost:** 2000 Poké Dollars (Moderate/Accessible).

## Effect & Mechanics
* **Primary Effect:** When setting up a picnic in the overworld, the player can select the Blue Bottle to change the visual arrangement of the table. It serves a purely cosmetic purpose and does not provide mechanical bonuses to Sandwich making, Meal Powers, or Egg Power.
* **Battle Attributes:**
    * **Fling Power:** N/A (This item cannot be held by a Pokémon in battle).
    * **Competitive Use:** None. This item is strictly for overworld flavor and customization; it has no utility in VGC, Smogon, or standard trainer battles.
* **Evolution/Interaction:** This item does not trigger any Pokémon evolutions and does not interact with abilities such as Pickup or Klutz.

## Acquisition & Lore
* **Availability:** The Blue Bottle is primarily available for purchase at **Picnic-Knacks** stores located in major Paldean hubs, including **Mesagoza**, **Cascarrafa**, and **Levincia**.
* **Wild Held Item:** This item is not held by wild Pokémon and cannot be farmed through battle encounters. It is strictly a retail-acquired customization piece.
