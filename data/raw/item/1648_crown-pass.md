## Identity & Utility
**Crown Pass** is a **Plot-Advancement (Key Item)** introduced in **Generation VIII** (Pokémon Sword & Shield).
* **Description:** A specialized travel permit that grants the player access to the Crown Tundra sub-region within the Galar region. It is a **Key Item** that cannot be consumed, sold, or held by a Pokémon. It is presented to the rail staff at Wedgehurst Station to initiate travel.
* **Cost:** 0 Poké Dollars (**Not for sale/Priceless**).

## Effect & Mechanics
* **Primary Effect:** Functions as a permanent gate-key for the "The Crown Tundra" expansion content. Once in the player's inventory, it unlocks the flight/train path from the Galar mainland to the Slippery Slope station in the Crown Tundra.
* **Battle Attributes:**
    * **Fling Power:** N/A (Cannot be held or flung in battle).
    * **Competitive Use:** **None.** This item exists solely for overworld progression and story advancement; it provides no tactical advantage in combat.
* **Evolution/Interaction:** This item does not trigger evolution or interact with any Pokémon abilities. It is strictly a "Plot-Advancement" tool.

## Acquisition & Lore
* **Availability:** Distributed automatically to players who have purchased the *Pokémon Sword Expansion Pass* or *Pokémon Shield Expansion Pass*. It typically appears in the player's inventory after arriving at Wedgehurst Station for the first time after the DLC is activated.
* **Wild Held Item:** This item is never held by wild Pokémon and cannot be obtained through the **Pickup** ability or any in-game farming method. It is a unique, one-per-save-file item.
