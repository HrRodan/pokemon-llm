## Identity & Utility
**Barred Cup** is a **Picnic** item introduced in **Generation IX (Pokémon Scarlet & Violet)**.
* **Description:** A decorative cup featuring a distinct barred pattern used to customize the player's picnic table. It is a permanent cosmetic item (stored in the Picnic Goods pocket) that can be purchased and reused indefinitely once acquired.
* **Cost:** 800 Poké Dollars (**Cheap/Accessible**).

## Effect & Mechanics
* **Primary Effect:** When the player sets up a picnic, they can choose to use the Barred Cup to change the visual appearance of the cups on the table. This is a purely cosmetic modification and does not influence the "Meal Powers" or the effectiveness of sandwiches.
* **Battle Attributes:**
    * **Fling Power:** N/A (Cannot be used in battle).
    * **Competitive Use:** **None.** This item has no impact on competitive play (VGC or Smogon) as it cannot be held by a Pokémon and provides no mechanical bonuses.
* **Evolution/Interaction:** Does not trigger evolution or interact with Pokémon abilities. It is exclusively for the Picnic mini-game/social feature.

## Acquisition & Lore
* **Availability:** Found in the **Paldea** region. It is primarily purchased at **Picnic-Knacks** specialty stores located in major hubs such as Mesagoza, Cascarrafa, and Levincia.
* **Wild Held Item:** This item is not held by wild Pokémon and cannot be obtained via the Pickup ability. It is strictly a retail-based cosmetic for the player's inventory.
