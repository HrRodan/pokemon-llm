## Identity & Utility
**Toedscool Flaps** is a **TM Material** introduced in **Generation IX (Pokémon Scarlet & Violet)**.
* **Description:** A dropped material shed by the Toedscool evolutionary line. It is a **Consumable Crafting Ingredient** that can be sold for a small amount of currency or used at a TM Machine. 
* **Cost:** 100 Poké Dollars (Cheap/Common). While it can be sold for Poké Dollars, its primary economic value is being exchanged for **League Points (LP)** or used as a component in move crafting.

## Effect & Mechanics
* **Primary Effect:** This item serves as a specific crafting component in the Paldea region. It is required to create certain **Technical Machines (TMs)** at the TM Machine located at Pokémon Centers.
* **Battle Attributes:**
    * **Fling Power:** N/A (This item cannot be effectively used in battle and provides no mechanical benefit if held).
    * **Competitive Use:** **None.** It is a utility item for character progression and move-pool expansion, holding no value in VGC or Smogon competitive formats.
* **Evolution/Interaction:** Does not trigger evolution. It is strictly a resource for the TM crafting system.

## Acquisition & Lore
* **Availability:** Obtained as a loot drop after defeating or catching wild Pokémon in the Toedscool family. It is a signature material for the "Woodear Pokémon" line.
* **Wild Held Item:** Not typically a held item; instead, it is automatically added to the player's inventory upon the defeat of wild **Toedscool** or **Toedscruel** in the Paldean wilderness. It is often gathered in bulk while exploring grassy or forest biomes where these Ground/Grass types reside.
