## Identity & Utility
**Storage Key (Galactic Warehouse)** is a **Key Item** (categorized as "unused" in the Gen VII engine) originally associated with the Sinnoh region.
* **Description:** This key provides access to Team Galactic’s sinister warehouse located on the outskirts of Veilstone City. As a Key Item, it is a permanent inventory addition for story progression and cannot be consumed, sold, or held by Pokémon.
* **Cost:** 0 Poké Dollars (Not for sale; acquired through story events).

## Effect & Mechanics
* **Primary Effect:** Unlocks the door to the Team Galactic Warehouse in Veilstone City. This is a critical story trigger that allows the player to navigate the Galactic HQ sub-complex, eventually leading to the recovery of HM02 (Fly) and progression toward the late-game confrontation with Cyrus.
* **Battle Attributes:**
    * **Fling Power:** N/A (Key Items cannot be held or used with Fling).
    * **Competitive Use:** None. This item exists solely for overworld progression and has no impact on competitive battling or VGC/Smogon formats.
* **Evolution/Interaction:** This item does not trigger any Pokémon evolutions and has no interaction with abilities like Pickup or Klutz.

## Acquisition & Lore
* **Availability:** In the games it originates from (Diamond, Pearl, Platinum, and their remakes), it is typically dropped by a Team Galactic Grunt outside the Veilstone Warehouse after the player has defeated Maylene and dealt with the Galactic presence in the city. In the Generation VII data provided, it is classified as "unused," indicating it is legacy data or a placeholder not obtainable through normal gameplay in the Alola region.
* **Wild Held Item:** Never found on wild Pokémon. It is a unique, one-of-a-kind quest item.
* **Lore Context:** The Galactic Warehouse serves as a front for Team Galactic’s operations in Veilstone City. The key is essential for uncovering the organization's plans regarding the Lake Guardians and the creation of the Red Chain.
