## Identity & Utility
**Liberty Pass** is an **Event-Item (Key Item)** introduced in **Generation V (Unova)**.
* **Description:** A special ticket used to board a ship at the Liberty Pier in Castelia City. It is a **Key Item** that **cannot** be bought or sold in shops.
* **Cost:** 0 Poké Dollars (**Not for sale/Key Item**).

## Effect & Mechanics
* **Primary Effect:** Grants the player access to a specific ship in Castelia City that travels to **Liberty Garden**. Upon arrival, the player can explore the island and encounter the Mythical Pokémon **Victini** (Level 15) inside the lighthouse.
* **Battle Attributes:**
    * **Fling Power:** N/A (Key Items cannot be held or used with the move Fling).
    * **Competitive Use:** **None.** This item has no utility in competitive battling; its purpose is strictly for world exploration and event-based Pokémon acquisition.
* **Evolution/Interaction:** This item is the prerequisite for the "Victini Event" in Pokémon Black and White. It does not trigger evolutions or interact with abilities like Pickup.

## Acquisition & Lore
* **Availability:** Originally distributed as a limited-time **Mystery Gift** via the Nintendo Wi-Fi Connection during the launch window of Pokémon Black and White. It is not obtainable through normal gameplay without an external event or distribution.
* **Wild Held Item:** This item is never held by wild Pokémon and cannot be gathered via the **Pickup** ability. It resides in the Key Items pocket of the Bag to prevent it from being traded or lost.
