## Identity & Utility
**Adventure Rules** is a Gameplay/Key Item introduced in **Generation VI (Kalos)**.
* **Description:** This item is a handmade instructional guidebook containing essential tips and points for novice Trainers. It serves as an in-game tutorial manual for basic exploration and mechanics. It is a **Key Item** that **cannot** be bought, sold, or discarded.
* **Cost:** 0 Poké Dollars (Not for sale; Unique story acquisition).

## Effect & Mechanics
* **Primary Effect:** When accessed from the bag, it displays basic gameplay information intended to help the player navigate the world of Pokémon.
* **Battle Attributes:**
    * **Fling Power:** N/A (Key Items cannot be held or used with the move Fling).
    * **Competitive Use:** **None.** This item has no battle application and is strictly for narrative and tutorial purposes during a playthrough.
* **Evolution/Interaction:** This item does not facilitate evolution and has no interaction with abilities like Pickup or Klutz.

## Acquisition & Lore
* **Availability:** This item is exclusive to **Pokémon X and Y**. It is given to the player by their friend/rival **Shauna** in Aquacorde Town early in the game as a gesture of friendship and support for their journey.
* **Wild Held Item:** Not available on wild Pokémon. It is a unique quest item limited to one per save file.
