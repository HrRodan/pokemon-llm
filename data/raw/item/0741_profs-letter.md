## Identity & Utility
**Prof's Letter** is a **plot-advancement** item introduced in **Generation VI (Kalos)**.
* **Description:** A formal letter written by Professor Sycamore addressed to the player's mother, characterized by a faint, pleasant perfume. It is a **Key Item** that functions as a narrative catalyst for the player to begin their journey. It **cannot** be bought or sold.
* **Cost:** 0 Poké Dollars (Key Item / Not for sale).

## Effect & Mechanics
* **Primary Effect:** The item acts as a delivery quest objective. When given to the player's mother in Vaniville Town, she grants the player a **Town Map** and a **Potion** in exchange for the news of their departure.
* **Battle Attributes:**
    * **Fling Power:** N/A (Key Items cannot be held by Pokémon or used with the move Fling).
    * **Competitive Use:** None. This item exists solely for story progression within the single-player campaign.
* **Evolution/Interaction:** This item does not facilitate evolution. It is a unique trigger for early-game progression, effectively serving as the "permission" for the protagonist to leave their hometown.

## Acquisition & Lore
* **Availability:** Obtained automatically during the early-game events of *Pokémon X* and *Pokémon Y*. It is handed to the player by their friends (Tierno and Shauna) in Aquacorde Town on behalf of the Professor.
* **Wild Held Item:** This item is never held by wild Pokémon. It is a unique, one-of-a-kind object within the Kalos region's narrative arc.
