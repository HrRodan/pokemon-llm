## Identity & Utility
**TM222 (Breaking Swipe)** is a **Technical Machine** introduced in **Generation IX** (specifically within the *Pokémon Scarlet and Violet: The Indigo Disk* DLC).
* **Description:** This item is a single-use teaching tool used to provide a Pokémon with the move **Breaking Swipe**. As a TM in the Paldea/Blueberry Academy ecosystem, it is a consumable item that can be reproduced via the TM Machine.
* **Cost:** 0 Poké Dollars (Not for sale). It is a **Special Acquisition** item obtained through exploration or crafting at the TM Machine using League Points (LP) and specific Pokémon materials.

## Effect & Mechanics
* **Primary Effect:** Teaches the move **Breaking Swipe** to a compatible Pokémon. 
    * **Move Profile:** A Physical Dragon-type attack with 60 base power.
    * **Secondary Effect:** It hits all adjacent opponents in Double Battles and is guaranteed (100% chance) to lower the Attack stat of all targets hit by one stage.
* **Battle Attributes:**
    * **Fling Power:** N/A (TMs cannot be used with the move Fling in current generations).
    * **Competitive Use:** **Highly Viable (VGC/Doubles).** Breaking Swipe is a premier utility move in competitive doubles formats. It provides consistent damage while simultaneously neutering physical attackers on the opposing side, making it a staple for bulky Dragon types or support-oriented Pokémon.
* **Evolution/Interaction:** This item does not trigger evolution. It is consumed upon use but the recipe remains unlocked at the TM Machine once found.

## Acquisition & Lore
* **Availability:** Found as a collectible item within the **Terarium** of Blueberry Academy (The Indigo Disk DLC). Once acquired, it can be crafted at any TM Machine.
* **Crafting Requirements:** To replicate TM222, a trainer typically requires:
    * **5,000 LP** (League Points)
    * **3x Axew Scales**
    * **3x Swablu Fluff**
* **Wild Held Item:** This item is never held by wild Pokémon; however, the materials required to craft it are gathered by defeating wild **Axew** and **Swablu** evolutionary lines.
