## Identity & Utility
**Data-Card-18** is a **Data Card** introduced in **Generation IV** (specifically appearing in *Pokémon HeartGold and SoulSilver*).
* **Description:** It is a specialized record-keeping item used to track a trainer's performance within the Pokéathlon. Specifically, this card displays the total number of first-place victories achieved in the **Circle Push** event. It functions as a **Key Item-style collectible** that cannot be used in battle or sold.
* **Cost:** 0 Poké Dollars (Not for sale; acquired via the Pokéathlon Dome's internal points system).

## Effect & Mechanics
* **Primary Effect:** Records and displays the cumulative number of times the trainer has come in first in the Pokéathlon’s **Circle Push** challenge.
* **Battle Attributes:**
    * **Fling Power:** N/A (This item cannot be held by a Pokémon and has no Fling effect).
    * **Competitive Use:** **None.** It is a purely cosmetic/stat-tracking item for side-activities and has no utility in VGC, Smogon, or standard trainer battles.
* **Evolution/Interaction:** This item does not facilitate evolution and does not interact with any Pokémon abilities (such as Pickup or Klutz).

## Acquisition & Lore
* **Availability:** This card is acquired at the **Pokéathlon Dome**, located in the Johto region (adjacent to the National Park). Trainers typically purchase Data Cards using **Athlete Points (AP)** earned by participating in various Pokéathlon competitions.
* **Wild Held Item:** Not available on any wild Pokémon. It is a unique piece of trainer equipment specifically tied to the "Power" category of the Pokéathlon minigames.
