## Identity & Utility
**Leafy-tablecloth** is a **Picnic** item introduced in **Generation IX (Pokémon Scarlet & Violet)**.
* **Description:** A decorative item used to customize the player's picnic setup. It features a nature-inspired design intended to change the aesthetic of the picnic table. It is a **Consumable/Permanent Aesthetic** item that **Can** be bought at specialized shops.
* **Cost:** 2000 Poké Dollars (Cheap/Standard acquisition).

## Effect & Mechanics
* **Primary Effect:** When the player initiates a Picnic, the Leafy-tablecloth can be selected to change the appearance of the table. This is a purely cosmetic effect and does not influence the "Egg Power" or "Meal Powers" of sandwiches made during the picnic.
* **Battle Attributes:**
    * **Fling Power:** N/A (Cannot be held or used in battle).
    * **Competitive Use:** None. This item is strictly for the **Overworld/Picnic** mechanic and has no utility in VGC, Smogon, or standard trainer battles.
* **Evolution/Interaction:** Does not trigger evolution or interact with Pokémon abilities. It serves as a collectible for players looking to complete their "Picnic Knacks" collection.

## Acquisition & Lore
* **Availability:** In the Paldea region, this item is primarily sold at **Picnic Knacks** stores located in major cities such as **Mesagoza**, **Cascarrafa**, and **Levincia**. 
* **Wild Held Item:** This item is not held by wild Pokémon and cannot be gathered via the **Pickup** ability; it is strictly a retail-purchased customization item.
