## Identity & Utility
**Coupon 2** is a **Plot-Advancement (Key Item)** introduced in **Generation IV (Sinnoh)**.
* **Description:** A promotional voucher intended for exchange at the Pokétch Company. It is one of three specific coupons required to obtain a Pokémon Watch (Pokétch). As a Key Item, it is stored in a separate pocket of the Bag and cannot be sold, traded, or given to a Pokémon to hold.
* **Cost:** 0 Poké Dollars (Not for sale/Unique Quest Item).

## Effect & Mechanics
* **Primary Effect:** When collected alongside Coupon 1 and Coupon 3, the player can exchange the set with the President of the Pokétch Company in Jubilife City to receive the **Pokétch** device. 
* **Battle Attributes:**
    * **Fling Power:** N/A (Key Items cannot be used with the move Fling).
    * **Competitive Use:** None. This item exists solely for story progression and unlocking out-of-battle utility features.
* **Evolution/Interaction:** This item does not facilitate Pokémon evolution. It is strictly used for the "Pokétch Campaign" event in the Sinnoh region.

## Acquisition & Lore
* **Availability:** Obtained in **Jubilife City** during the early stages of *Pokémon Diamond, Pearl, Platinum*, and their remakes *Brilliant Diamond and Shining Pearl*. 
* **Quest Details:** To acquire Coupon 2, the player must find one of three Clowns hidden throughout Jubilife City and answer a basic Pokémon trivia question correctly (Question: "Does a Pokémon grow by defeating others and gaining Exp. Points?").
* **Wild Held Item:** This item is never held by wild Pokémon; it is a unique event item provided by an NPC.
* **Lore Context:** The Pokétch Company uses these coupons as part of a marketing campaign to promote their multi-functional watch. This quest serves as an early-game tutorial to encourage exploration of Sinnoh's first major city.
