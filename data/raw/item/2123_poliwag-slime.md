## Identity & Utility
**Poliwag Slime** is a **TM Material** introduced in **Generation IX (Pokémon Scarlet & Violet)**.
* **Description:** This item is a specialized crafting reagent representing a secretion from the Poliwag evolutionary line. It is a **Consumable Crafting Material** that can be sold for a small amount of currency or used at a TM Machine.
* **Cost:** 35 Poké Dollars/League Points (**Cheap/Common reagent**).

## Effect & Mechanics
* **Primary Effect:** Used exclusively as a component for crafting Technical Machines (TMs) at the TM Machine found at Pokémon Centers. It is consumed alongside League Points (LP) to create specific move-teaching discs.
* **Battle Attributes:**
    * **Fling Power:** N/A (Cannot be held or used in battle).
    * **Competitive Use:** **None.** This item has no tactical utility in competitive VGC or Smogon formats; its value is strictly limited to team preparation and move-set customization during the preparation phase.
* **Evolution/Interaction:** This item does not trigger evolution. It is categorized under the "TM Materials" pocket of the bag and does not interact with abilities like Pickup or Klutz in a combat capacity.

## Acquisition & Lore
* **Availability:** Obtained as a drop after defeating or catching wild members of the Poliwag evolutionary line.
* **Wild Drop Source:** Primarily gathered by battling **Poliwag**, **Poliwhirl**, **Poliwrath**, and **Politoed** (introduced via the *Teal Mask* DLC in the Paldea/Kitakami ecosystems). It is often farmed efficiently using the "Let's Go!" auto-battle feature.
