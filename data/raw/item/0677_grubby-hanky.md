## Identity & Utility
**Grubby Hanky** is a **Plot-advancement (Key Item)** introduced in **Generation V (Unova Region)**.
* **Description:** A handkerchief dropped by a regular patron at the Café Warehouse. It carries a faint scent of a Pokémon, hinting at its owner's companion. As a Key Item, it is a non-consumable quest object that cannot be sold, traded, or held by a Pokémon.
* **Cost:** 0 Poké Dollars (Not for sale; Special Acquisition).

## Effect & Mechanics
* **Primary Effect:** Serves as a specific quest trigger within the Unova region (specifically in *Pokémon Black 2 and White 2*). The player must locate the item on a specific day of the week to initiate a delivery event.
* **Battle Attributes:**
    * **Fling Power:** N/A (Key Items cannot be used with the move Fling).
    * **Competitive Use:** **None.** This item has no mechanical impact on Pokémon battles, stats, or competitive play. It is strictly for overworld flavor and side-quest completion.
* **Evolution/Interaction:** Does not facilitate Pokémon evolution. Its only interaction is with a specific NPC who owns a **Patrat**.

## Acquisition & Lore
* **Availability:** This item is highly time-sensitive. It appears on the floor of the **Café Warehouse** in **Castelia City** specifically on **Sundays**. 
* **Quest Context:** To complete the interaction, the player must hold the item and return it to the original owner—a customer who appears at the same café accompanied by a Patrat—on a **Thursday**.
* **Wild Held Item:** This item is never held by wild Pokémon and cannot be obtained through the **Pickup** ability. It is a unique, fixed-location quest item.
