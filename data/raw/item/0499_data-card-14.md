## Identity & Utility
**Data Card 14** is a **Data Card** (Key Item) introduced in **Generation IV** (specifically appearing in *Pokémon HeartGold and SoulSilver*).
* **Description:** It is a specialized record-keeping device used exclusively for the Pokéathlon mini-games. This card specifically tracks the cumulative number of times the trainer's Pokémon have successfully performed a "Tackle" during Pokéathlon events. It is a **Key Item** that **cannot be bought** with standard currency.
* **Cost:** 0 Poké Dollars (**Not for sale**; acquired via special exchange).

## Effect & Mechanics
* **Primary Effect:** Records and displays the total number of times the trainer's Pokémon have tackled opponents in the Pokéathlon. This serves as a statistical tracker for completionists and players aiming to maximize their Pokéathlon records.
* **Battle Attributes:**
    * **Fling Power:** N/A (Key Items cannot be held or used with Fling).
    * **Competitive Use:** **None.** This item exists solely for the Pokéathlon side-activity and has no impact on turn-based battles, VGC, or Smogon formats.
* **Evolution/Interaction:** Does not evolve any Pokémon. It interacts solely with the Pokéathlon data terminal located in the Pokéathlon Dome.

## Acquisition & Lore
* **Availability:** This item is obtained at the **Pokéathlon Dome** (located north of Goldenrod City in the Johto region). It is typically purchased from the Athlete Shop using **Athlete Points (AP)**, which are earned by participating in various Pokéathlon events.
* **Wild Held Item:** This item is never held by wild Pokémon and cannot be obtained through the **Pickup** ability. It is a unique, player-specific tracking tool.
