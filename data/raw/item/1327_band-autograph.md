## Identity & Utility
**Band Autograph** is a **Souvenir / Key Item** introduced in **Generation VIII** (Galar Region).
* **Description:** An autograph signed by all members of the Maximizers, a charismatic band popular within the Galar region. It serves as a commemorative collectible rather than a functional tool. It is a **Key Item** that **cannot** be bought or sold.
* **Cost:** 0 Poké Dollars (**Not for sale**).

## Effect & Mechanics
* **Primary Effect:** The Band Autograph has **no mechanical effect** in battle, evolution, or overworld navigation. Its primary purpose is flavor and completionism within the player's Bag.
* **Battle Attributes:**
    * **Fling Power:** N/A (Key Items cannot be held or used with Fling).
    * **Competitive Use:** **None.** This item has no impact on competitive play (VGC/Smogon) and cannot be held by a Pokémon.
* **Evolution/Interaction:** This item does not trigger any Pokémon evolutions and does not interact with abilities like Pickup or Klutz.

## Acquisition & Lore
* **Availability:** This is a special event/interaction item found in **Pokémon Sword and Shield**. It is typically obtained by speaking to specific NPCs associated with the band "The Maximizers" (the band that performs the game's ending theme) in locations like Motostoke or during post-game events.
* **Wild Held Item:** This item is **not held by any wild Pokémon**. It is a unique item granted to the player through dialogue or scripted events.
* **Lore context:** The Maximizers are the in-universe band responsible for the high-energy music found in the Galar region's stadium-centric culture. This autograph represents the player's status as a champion or a recognized figure in Galar's celebrity-driven society.
