## Identity & Utility
**Red Flag Pick** is a **Picnic** item introduced in **Generation IX (Pokémon Scarlet & Violet)**.
*   **Description:** A small, decorative red flag pick used during the sandwich-making process at Picnics. It serves as the final component to secure a completed sandwich. It is a **Consumable** item that can be bought in bulk.
*   **Cost:** 120 Poké Dollars (**Cheap/Accessible**).

## Effect & Mechanics
*   **Primary Effect:** Used during the Sandwich-making minigame. After placing ingredients and the top bun, the player must place a pick to complete the sandwich. While largely cosmetic, the pick is a required component to finalize the sandwich and receive its "Meal Powers" (e.g., Sparkling Power, Encounter Power).
*   **Battle Attributes:**
    *   **Fling Power:** N/A (This item cannot be held in battle and has no Fling effect).
    *   **Competitive Use:** **None.** This item exists solely for the overworld Picnic mechanic and has no utility in VGC, Smogon, or standard trainer battles.
*   **Evolution/Interaction:** Does not evolve any Pokémon. It is consumed upon the successful completion of a sandwich.

## Acquisition & Lore
*   **Availability:** Readily available for purchase at specialized food and supply shops throughout the Paldea region, most notably at **Sure Cans**, **Every Wich Way**, and **Aguilar's Supermarket**.
*   **Wild Held Item:** This item is not held by wild Pokémon. It is a mass-produced consumer good for trainers participating in the Paldean picnic culture.
