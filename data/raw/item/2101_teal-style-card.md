## Identity & Utility
**Teal Style Card** is a **Key Item** (classified as Gameplay) introduced in **Generation IX** (Pokémon Scarlet and Violet: *The Teal Mask* DLC).
* **Description:** A special permit card that identifies the holder as a distinguished visitor to the Kitakami region. It serves as a permanent unlock for expanded customization options. It is a **Key Item** that **cannot** be bought or sold.
* **Cost:** 0 Poké Dollars (**Not for sale/Unique acquisition**).

## Effect & Mechanics
* **Primary Effect:** Upon acquisition, the Teal Style Card automatically expands the inventory of boutiques and hair salons across both the Paldea region and the land of Kitakami. It unlocks a wide array of new clothing items, accessories, and hairstyles that were previously unavailable.
* **Battle Attributes:**
    * **Fling Power:** N/A (Key Items cannot be held or used with the move Fling).
    * **Competitive Use:** **None.** This item exists purely for player customization and "Fashion Souls" aesthetic progression. It has no impact on battle stats, typing, or move pools.
* **Evolution/Interaction:** This item does not facilitate evolution. It interacts solely with NPC shopkeepers and salon stylists.

## Acquisition & Lore
* **Availability:** The Teal Style Card is obtained as part of the main storyline in the **The Teal Mask** expansion. It is gifted to the player by the **Caretaker** in Mossui Town after the player completes specific tasks related to the festival and the history of the "Loyal Three" and Ogerpon.
* **Wild Held Item:** None. As a unique Key Item, it cannot be found on wild Pokémon or gathered via the **Pickup** ability. It is tied to the player's character progression as a mark of their integration into Kitakami culture.
