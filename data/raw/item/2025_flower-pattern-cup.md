## Identity & Utility
**Flower-pattern-cup** is a **Picnic** item introduced in **Generation IX (Pokémon Scarlet & Violet)**.
* **Description:** A cosmetic customization accessory used to decorate the player's picnic set. It replaces the standard picnic cups with a floral-themed variant. It is a **Permanent Customization** item that is purchased once and stored in the Picnic Knacks bag.
* **Cost:** 800 Poké Dollars (Cheap/Accessible).

## Effect & Mechanics
* **Primary Effect:** Purely aesthetic. When active in the Picnic menu, it changes the visual model of the cups placed on the table during the sandwich-making minigame and social interactions with Pokémon.
* **Battle Attributes:**
    * **Fling Power:** N/A (Cannot be held by a Pokémon or used in battle).
    * **Competitive Use:** **None.** This item has no impact on stats, typing, or move pools. It is strictly a "flavor" item for the overworld picnic mechanic.
* **Evolution/Interaction:** This item does not facilitate evolution and does not interact with abilities such as Pickup or Klutz. It is part of the "Picnic Knacks" sub-category of items used to personalize the Paldean camping experience.

## Acquisition & Lore
* **Availability:** Primarily sold at **Picnic Knacks** specialty shops located in major Paldean hubs, including **Mesagoza**, **Cascarrafa**, and **Levincia**. 
* **Wild Held Item:** Not found on wild Pokémon. It is a commercially produced item for trainers, emphasizing the lifestyle and bonding aspects of the Pokémon journey in the Paldea region.
