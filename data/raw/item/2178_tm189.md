## Identity & Utility
**TM189 (Meteor Beam)** is a **Technical Machine (TM)** introduced in **Generation IX** (Pokémon Scarlet & Violet: The Indigo Disk).
* **Description:** This machine teaches the move **Meteor Beam**, a powerful Rock-type Special attack. As a TM in the Paldea/Blueberry Academy region, it is a single-use consumable that can be crafted repeatedly once unlocked. It is a **Specialized Training Item** that cannot be purchased for traditional Poké Dollars.
* **Cost:** 0 Poké Dollars (**Not for sale**; requires League Points and Pokémon Materials to craft).

## Effect & Mechanics
* **Primary Effect:** Teaches the move **Meteor Beam** to a compatible Pokémon. 
    * **Move Statistics:** Meteor Beam is a **Rock-type Special Move** with **120 Base Power** and **90% Accuracy**. 
    * **Mechanic:** It is a two-turn move. On the first turn, the user gathers space energy and **raises its Special Attack by one stage**. On the second turn, it deals massive damage.
* **Battle Attributes:**
    * **Fling Power:** N/A (TMs cannot be used with the move Fling).
    * **Competitive Use:** **High (Competitive Staple).** Meteor Beam is frequently paired with the **Power Herb** held item. This allows the Pokémon to gain the Special Attack boost and fire off the 120-power nuke in a single turn. It is a premier choice for Special Attackers like **Glimmora**, **Nihilego**, and **Minior** to gain momentum while dealing heavy damage.
* **Evolution/Interaction:** This item does not trigger evolution. It is part of the expanded TM list (172-201) added in the Generation IX DLC.

## Acquisition & Lore
* **Availability:** Obtained in the **Blueberry Academy (The Indigo Disk)**. It can be found as a collectible item in the Terarium (specifically the Canyon Biome) or unlocked for crafting at any TM Machine.
* **Crafting Requirements:** To replicate this TM, the player typically needs:
    * **8,000 LP (League Points)**
    * **Minior Chips (x5)**
    * **Glimmet Crystals (x3)**
* **Wild Held Item:** Not found on wild Pokémon. It is exclusively acquired through exploration or the TM crafting system using materials gathered from wild **Minior** and **Glimmet**.
