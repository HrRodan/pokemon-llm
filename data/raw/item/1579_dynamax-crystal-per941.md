## Identity & Utility
**Dynamax Crystal (PER941)** is a **Dynamax Crystal** introduced in **Generation VIII (Galar)**.
* **Description:** A specialized consumable item used to trigger a specific Max Raid Battle at the Watchtower Lair in the Galar Wild Area. Once activated, the designated Pokémon appears in the den until the end of the current day. It is a **Consumable Event Item** that cannot be purchased through standard gameplay.
* **Cost:** 20 Poké Dollars (**Special Acquisition**; nominal cost assigned to promotional items).

## Effect & Mechanics
* **Primary Effect:** When used at the **Watchtower Lair** (an empty den in the Wild Area), it forces a Max Raid Battle against a specific Pokémon species. This encounter remains active until midnight of the day it was activated. 
* **Battle Attributes:**
    * **Fling Power:** N/A (This item cannot be used with the move Fling).
    * **Competitive Use:** **None.** This item exists outside of the competitive battle loop. Its primary value is for collectors and competitive players to encounter specific Pokémon that may have Hidden Abilities, high IVs, or Gigantamax factors that are otherwise difficult to obtain.
* **Evolution/Interaction:** This item does not evolve Pokémon. It interacts exclusively with the **Max Raid Den** mechanics in *Pokémon Sword & Shield*. It is consumed upon use.

## Acquisition & Lore
* **Availability:** Dynamax Crystals are typically distributed via **Serial Codes** as part of retail promotions (e.g., GameStop, Target, or pre-order bonuses). They are not available in Pokémon Marts or through natural exploration.
* **Wild Held Item:** This item is never held by wild Pokémon and cannot be gathered via the **Pickup** ability. It is a strictly external distribution item intended for special event encounters.
