## Identity & Utility
**Data-card-24** is a **Data Card** introduced in **Generation IV** (specifically appearing in *Pokémon HeartGold and SoulSilver*).
* **Description:** This is a specialized data-tracking tool used to archive specific performance metrics within the Pokéathlon. It specifically tracks the number of times a Trainer has secured first place in the **Goal Roll** mini-game. It is a **Key Item / Data Card** that cannot be bought with standard currency.
* **Cost:** 0 Poké Dollars (**Not for sale**; acquired via special points).

## Effect & Mechanics
* **Primary Effect:** Records and displays the trainer's "First Place" win count for the Pokéathlon's **Goal Roll** event. It serves as a permanent record within the Pokéathlon Dome’s data systems.
* **Battle Attributes:**
    * **Fling Power:** N/A (Cannot be held or used in battle).
    * **Competitive Use:** **None.** This is a purely cosmetic/completionist item for the Pokéathlon side-activity and has no impact on competitive Pokémon battling (VGC or Smogon).
* **Evolution/Interaction:** This item does not evolve any Pokémon. It functions exclusively as a record-keeping device and does not interact with abilities like Pickup or Klutz.

## Acquisition & Lore
* **Availability:** In *Pokémon HeartGold and SoulSilver*, this card is purchased from the **Pokéathlon Dome's Athlete Shop** for 500 Athlete Points (AP). It only becomes available for purchase on specific days of the week (typically **Tuesdays and Fridays**).
* **Wild Held Item:** This item is never held by wild Pokémon. It is a unique data record tied to the player's achievements in the Johto region's Pokéathlon competitions.
