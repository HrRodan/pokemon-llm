## Identity & Utility
**Academy-tablecloth** is a **Picnic** item introduced in **Generation IX (Paldea)**.
* **Description:** A specialized decorative cloth used to customize the appearance of the table during a Picnic. It typically features the branding and colors associated with the Naranja or Uva Academy. It is a **Special/Key Item** that **cannot** be bought at standard Poke Marts.
* **Cost:** 0 Poké Dollars (**Not for sale**; usually obtained via specific NPC interactions or as a starting accessory).

## Effect & Mechanics
* **Primary Effect:** When the player initiates a Picnic, they can select this item from the "Tablecloths" menu to change the visual aesthetic of the picnic table. This change is purely **cosmetic** and does not impact the quality of Sandwiches or the duration of Meal Powers.
* **Battle Attributes:**
    * **Fling Power:** N/A (This item cannot be held by a Pokémon and cannot be used in battle).
    * **Competitive Use:** **None**. This is an overworld-only cosmetic item with no impact on Pokémon stats, moves, or competitive viability.
* **Evolution/Interaction:** This item does not facilitate the evolution of any Pokémon. It interacts exclusively with the **Picnic mechanic** introduced in Pokémon Scarlet and Violet.

## Acquisition & Lore
* **Availability:** Distributed to the player at the **Academy in Mesagoza** or obtained from the Picnic Knacks NPC in various towns across Paldea. 
* **Wild Held Item:** This item is never held by wild Pokémon. It is a unique player accessory that cannot be farmed via the Pickup ability or Thief/Covet.
* **Lore context:** As part of the Academy's extracurricular "Treasure Hunt," students are provided with basic gear, including this tablecloth, to facilitate bonding with their Pokémon in the field.
