## Identity & Utility
**Sonia's Book** is a **Plot-Advancement (Key Item)** introduced in **Generation VIII** (Galar Region).
* **Description:** This item consists of Professor Sonia’s published research and writings regarding the legends of the Galar region, specifically focusing on the Zacian, Zamazenta, and the Darkest Day. It is a **Key Item** that cannot be consumed, sold, or held by a Pokémon.
* **Cost:** 0 Poké Dollars (**Key Item/Special Acquisition**).

## Effect & Mechanics
* **Primary Effect:** Serves as a narrative tool and a symbol of completion for Sonia's character arc. In the context of the game's mechanics, it is used to advance the post-game storyline in *Pokémon Sword and Shield*. It provides lore context for players interested in the historical background of the Galar region.
* **Battle Attributes:**
    * **Fling Power:** N/A (Key Items cannot be used with the move Fling).
    * **Competitive Use:** **None.** This item has no mechanical impact on competitive battling (VGC or Smogon) and cannot be equipped to a Pokémon.
* **Evolution/Interaction:** This item does not trigger evolution or interact with abilities like Pickup or Klutz. Its sole purpose is narrative progression and world-building.

## Acquisition & Lore
* **Availability:** This is a unique item obtained during the post-game of *Pokémon Sword and Shield*. It is given to the player by **Professor Sonia** herself after she achieves the rank of Professor and publishes her findings on the true history of Galar's heroes.
* **Wild Held Item:** This item is never held by wild Pokémon. It is a unique story-related object tied specifically to the player's progress in the Galar region.
