## Identity & Utility
**Yellow Bell Pepper** is a **Sandwich Ingredient** introduced in **Generation IX** (Pokémon Scarlet/Violet).
* **Description:** A crisp and colorful sliced vegetable used specifically for the Sandwich-making mechanic during Picnics. It is a consumable ingredient that is readily available for purchase at grocery vendors throughout the Paldea region.
* **Cost:** 240 Poké Dollars (Cheap/Accessible).

## Effect & Mechanics
* **Primary Effect:** When used in a Sandwich, it contributes specific values to **Meal Powers**. It primarily provides points toward **Electric-type** boosts. It is a versatile ingredient used in various recipes to increase the levels of Title Power, Encounter Power, and Raid Power.
* **Battle Attributes:**
    * **Fling Power:** N/A (This item cannot be held by a Pokémon in battle and thus cannot be used with the move Fling).
    * **Competitive Use:** While it has no direct utility in VGC or Smogon formats, it is a **Metagame Essential** for Shiny Hunting, Breeding (Egg Power), and EV Training optimization through the Picnic mechanic.
* **Evolution/Interaction:** Does not trigger evolution. Its primary interaction is with the Gen IX **Picnic/Sandwich** crafting system.

## Acquisition & Lore
* **Availability:** Can be purchased from **Deli Cioso** and **Aquiesta Supermarket** locations across the Paldea region. These shops typically unlock more ingredients as the player earns more Gym Badges.
* **Wild Held Item:** Not found on wild Pokémon. It is strictly a commercial food item gathered by trainers via retail shops for culinary use.
