## Identity & Utility
**Slowpoke Cup** is a **Picnic Item** introduced in **Generation IX (Pokémon Scarlet & Violet)**.
* **Description:** A decorative accessory used to customize the player's picnic set. It features a design inspired by the Pokémon Slowpoke. It is a **Consumable (Static Decoration)** that can be bought at specialty shops.
* **Cost:** 2000 Poké Dollars (**Cheap/Accessible**).

## Effect & Mechanics
* **Primary Effect:** When the player initiates a Picnic, the Slowpoke Cup replaces the default cups on the table. It provides a cosmetic change to the picnic environment but offers no direct mechanical bonuses to Sandwich making or Pokémon stats.
* **Battle Attributes:**
    * **Fling Power:** N/A (Cannot be held or used in battle).
    * **Competitive Use:** **None.** This is a purely aesthetic item used within the overworld "Picnic" feature and has no utility in VGC, Smogon, or standard battle formats.
* **Evolution/Interaction:** Does not trigger evolution or interact with any Pokémon abilities. Its presence is strictly visual for the player's customized picnic layout.

## Acquisition & Lore
* **Availability:** Commonly purchased at **Picnic Knacks** stores located in major Paldean hubs such as **Mesagoza, Cascarrafa, and Levincia**. 
* **Wild Held Item:** This item is never held by wild Pokémon; it is a manufactured consumer good within the Paldea region's economy.
* **World Context:** Part of a series of "Slowpoke" branded merchandise in the Pokémon world, reinforcing the franchise's internal consistency where popular Pokémon are used as motifs for household items.
