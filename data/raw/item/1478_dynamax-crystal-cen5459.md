## Identity & Utility
**Dynamax Crystal ★Cen5459** is a **Dynamax Crystal** introduced in **Generation VIII (Sword & Shield)**.
* **Description:** A rare, promotional consumable used to trigger a specific Max Raid Battle at the Watchtower Lair in Galar's Wild Area. Once activated, the encounter remains available until the end of the day. It is a single-use event item that cannot be purchased through standard in-game vendors.
* **Cost:** 20 Poké Dollars (Cheap/Placeholder; technically priceless as it is a promotional distribution item).

## Effect & Mechanics
* **Primary Effect:** When used at the specific **Watchtower Lair** (located in the Watchtower Ruins of the Wild Area), it causes a red beam of light to appear, initiating a Max Raid Battle. For this specific crystal (Cen5459), it typically triggers an encounter with a **Gigantamax Centiskorch**.
* **Battle Attributes:**
    * **Fling Power:** N/A (This item cannot be held or used with the move Fling).
    * **Competitive Use:** N/A. While the item itself has no competitive battle function, it is used by players to capture Pokémon with specific Gigantamax factors, high Individual Values (IVs), or Hidden Abilities for use in VGC and Smogon formats.
* **Evolution/Interaction:** Does not evolve Pokémon. It serves as a "summoning" mechanic for the Max Raid Den system.

## Acquisition & Lore
* **Availability:** This is an event-exclusive item. It was primarily distributed via **serial codes** through retail partnerships (such as GAME in the UK or GameStop) or as a bonus for specific purchases (like the Sword & Shield Expansion Pass or Dual Pack). It is not available through normal gameplay or Marts.
* **Wild Held Item:** This item is never held by wild Pokémon. It is categorized as a special event distribution item, distinct from standard medicines or held items.
