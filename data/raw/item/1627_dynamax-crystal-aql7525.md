## Identity & Utility
**Dynamax Crystal (aql7525)** is a **Dynamax Crystal** introduced in **Generation VIII (Pokémon Sword & Shield)**.
* **Description:** This is a rare, consumable event item used specifically at the Watchtower Lair in the Galar Wild Area. Once consumed, it triggers a Max Raid Battle that persists until the end of the day. It is a **Special Event/Consumable** item that cannot be bought through standard gameplay.
* **Cost:** 20 Poké Dollars (**Not for sale**; this nominal cost is a placeholder for a promotional item distributed via serial codes).

## Effect & Mechanics
* **Primary Effect:** When used at the Watchtower Lair (located in the Watchtower Ruins of the Wild Area), it causes a specific Pokémon—in this case, **Gigantamax Centiskorch**—to appear as a Max Raid Battle boss. 
* **Battle Attributes:**
    * **Fling Power:** N/A (This item cannot be held by a Pokémon or used with the move Fling).
    * **Competitive Use:** **None.** This item exists purely for overworld utility to facilitate the capture of high-IV or Gigantamax-capable Pokémon for the player's roster.
* **Evolution/Interaction:** Does not evolve Pokémon. It interacts exclusively with the **Watchtower Lair** mechanics in the Galar region.

## Acquisition & Lore
* **Availability:** This item was not available for purchase in Poké Marts. It was distributed as a **Limited-Time Serial Code** promotion, often tied to retail partnerships (such as GameStop, Best Buy, or international hobby shops) or through the purchase of specific Pokémon Trading Card Game (TCG) products. 
* **Wild Held Item:** None. This item is never found on wild Pokémon and cannot be gathered via the **Pickup** ability. It is strictly an event-exclusive trigger for Max Raid Battles.
