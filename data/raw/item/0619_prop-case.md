## Identity & Utility
**Prop Case** is a **Gameplay (Key Item)** introduced in **Generation V (Unova)**.
* **Description:** A specialized storage container used to hold "Props"—various colorful accessories and dress-up items used to customize Pokémon for performances. It is a **Key Item** that **cannot** be bought or sold.
* **Cost:** 0 Poké Dollars (**Not for sale**).

## Effect & Mechanics
* **Primary Effect:** Acts as a dedicated inventory for Props. It allows players to collect, carry, and manage the decorative items required for the **Pokémon Musical** minigame found in Nimbasa City.
* **Battle Attributes:**
    * **Fling Power:** N/A (Key Items cannot be used with the move Fling).
    * **Competitive Use:** **None.** This item has no impact on battle mechanics, stats, or competitive play. Its utility is strictly limited to the cosmetic side-activity of the Pokémon Musical.
* **Evolution/Interaction:** This item does not trigger evolution or interact with battle-oriented abilities like Pickup or Klutz. It is purely a functional container for the player's fashion inventory.

## Acquisition & Lore
* **Availability:** In *Pokémon Black, White, Black 2, and White 2*, the Prop Case is given to the player by the owner of the **Musical Theater** in **Nimbasa City** upon their first visit.
* **Wild Held Item:** This item is never held by wild Pokémon. It is a unique, one-of-a-kind Key Item central to the Unova region's culture of Pokémon showmanship.
