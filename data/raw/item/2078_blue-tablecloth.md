## Identity & Utility
**Blue-tablecloth** is a **Picnic** item introduced in **Generation IX (Paldea)**.
* **Description:** A cosmetic customization item used to change the visual appearance of the player's picnic table. It is a **permanent cosmetic item** that can be bought and swapped at will while setting up a picnic in the overworld.
* **Cost:** 5000 Poké Dollars (**Moderate/Accessible**).

## Effect & Mechanics
* **Primary Effect:** When the player initiates a Picnic, they can interact with the table to change its appearance. Selecting the Blue Tablecloth replaces the default cloth with a blue-colored variant. It provides no mechanical bonuses to Sandwich making or Egg discovery rates.
* **Battle Attributes:**
    * **Fling Power:** N/A (This item cannot be held by a Pokémon and has no Fling utility).
    * **Competitive Use:** **None.** It is strictly a social and aesthetic feature used for flavor and screen-sharing during the picnic mini-game.
* **Evolution/Interaction:** This item does not trigger any evolutions nor does it interact with Pokémon abilities like Pickup or Klutz.

## Acquisition & Lore
* **Availability:** It is primarily sold at **Picnic-Knacks** stores throughout the Paldea region, specifically in major hubs like **Mesagoza**, **Cascarrafa**, and **Levincia**. 
* **Wild Held Item:** This item is never held by wild Pokémon and cannot be gathered via the Pickup ability. It must be purchased from specialized vendors catering to outdoor enthusiasts.
