## Identity & Utility
**TM134 (Reversal)** is a **Technical Machine** introduced in **Generation IX (Scarlet & Violet)**.
* **Description:** A specialized move-teaching device used to grant a Pokémon the Fighting-type move **Reversal**. In the Paldea region, TMs are single-use consumable items that are crafted at a TM Machine or found in the overworld.
* **Cost:** 0 Poké Dollars (Not for sale at standard Marts; requires **3,000 LP** and specific Pokémon materials to craft).

## Effect & Mechanics
* **Primary Effect:** Teaches the move **Reversal**. Reversal is a Physical Fighting-type move that deals more damage the lower the user's remaining HP. 
    * *Power Scaling:* 20 Base Power (at >68% HP) up to a maximum of **200 Base Power** (at <4.17% HP).
* **Battle Attributes:**
    * **Fling Power:** N/A (Technical Machines generally cannot be Flung for damage in the current generation).
    * **Competitive Use:** This is a **Niche/High-Risk utility item**. It is most effective on "Endure" or "Focus Sash" sets, allowing a Pokémon to survive at 1 HP and strike back with a maximum power 200 BP Fighting STAB move. Common users include Lucario, Heracross, and Falinks.
* **Evolution/Interaction:** Does not trigger evolution. It is compatible with a wide variety of Fighting-types and fast physical attackers with the "Glass Cannon" archetype.

## Acquisition & Lore
* **Availability:** Specifically available in **Pokémon Scarlet & Violet**. It can be crafted at any Pokémon Center TM Machine once the recipe is unlocked (usually by finding the TM in the overworld or defeating specific Team Star bosses).
* **Crafting Ingredients:**
    * 3,000 League Points (LP)
    * 3x **Falinks Sweat**
    * 3x **Mankey Fur**
* **Wild Held Item:** Not found on wild Pokémon. It is exclusively obtained through crafting, overworld exploration, or as a reward for Battle Studies at the Naranja/Uva Academy.
