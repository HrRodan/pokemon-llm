## Identity & Utility
**TM97 (Dark Pulse)** is a **Technical Machine (TM)** introduced in **Generation VI** (Kalos).
* **Description:** This item teaches a Pokémon the move **Dark Pulse**, a powerful Dark-type attack where the user releases a "horrible aura imbued with dark thoughts." It is a machine used to expand a Pokémon's move pool and [Can] be bought in various regions.
* **Cost:** 1,000 Poké Dollars (Cheap/Accessible).

## Effect & Mechanics
* **Primary Effect:** Teaches the move **Dark Pulse** to a compatible Pokémon. In the context of the move's mechanics:
    * **Type:** Dark / Special
    * **Power:** 80
    * **Accuracy:** 100%
    * **Secondary Effect:** 20% chance to make the target flinch.
* **Battle Attributes:**
    * **Fling Power:** N/A (Technical Machines cannot be used with the move Fling).
    * **Competitive Use:** **Competitive Staple.** Dark Pulse is the primary Special-category Dark-type move for most competitive sets. It is frequently seen on Pokémon like Hydreigon, Darkrai, Gengar (for coverage), and Greninja. The flinch chance makes it particularly effective on fast "sweeper" Pokémon.
* **Evolution/Interaction:** Does not trigger evolution. Between Generation V and Generation VII, TMs were infinite-use; however, in recent iterations (like Generation IX), they have returned to being single-use consumables that can be crafted at a TM Machine.

## Acquisition & Lore
* **Availability:** TM97 is widely available across multiple regions. In **X/Y**, it is found in Route 15 (Hôtel Désolation); in **Omega Ruby/Alpha Sapphire**, it is located in the Team Magma/Aqua Hideout; in **Sword/Shield**, it is found in the Wild Area (Lake of Outrage).
* **Wild Held Item:** This item is not held by wild Pokémon. It is typically acquired as a static overworld pickup or purchased from specialized vendors such as the Technical Machine clerks in Pokémon Centers or the Battle Frontier.
