## Identity & Utility
**Bombirdier Feather** is a **TM Material** introduced in **Generation IX (Paldea)**.
* **Description:** A clump of feathers dropped by the Pokémon Bombirdier. As a member of the "tm-materials" category, it is a resource specifically used at the Technical Machine Machine (TM Machine) located at Pokémon Centers. It is a consumable crafting component that cannot be bought from shops.
* **Cost:** 200 Poké Dollars (Cheap/Standard sell price for crafting materials).

## Effect & Mechanics
* **Primary Effect:** Serves as a necessary ingredient for crafting specific Technical Machines (TMs). In *Pokémon Scarlet and Violet*, this material is required to create high-tier Flying-type and utility moves.
* **Battle Attributes:**
    * **Fling Power:** N/A (This item cannot be used in battle and has no Fling effect).
    * **Competitive Use:** None. Its utility is strictly limited to the overworld crafting system and the in-game economy.
* **Evolution/Interaction:** This item does not trigger any evolutions. Its sole purpose is its role in the Gen IX crafting recipe system. Common recipes include **TM097 (Fly)**, **TM164 (Brave Bird)**, and **TM113 (Tailwind)**.

## Acquisition & Lore
* **Availability:** Obtained as a drop after defeating or catching wild **Bombirdier**. 
* **Wild Held Item:** While not a traditional held item, it is guaranteed loot from wild **Bombirdier**. These Pokémon are commonly found in the mountainous and coastal regions of the **West Province (Area One)** and **South Province (Area Six)** in the Paldea region. Gathering this item is essential for trainers looking to teach powerful Flying-type moves to their team via the TM Machine.
