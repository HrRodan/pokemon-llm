## Identity & Utility
**Battle-tablecloth** is a **Picnic Item** introduced in **Generation IX (Paldea)**.
* **Description:** A specialized cosmetic item used to customize the appearance of the player's picnic table. It features a design inspired by battle motifs. It is a **Reusable Cosmetic** item that can be purchased at specific retail outlets.
* **Cost:** 4,000 Poké Dollars (**Moderate/Accessible**).

## Effect & Mechanics
* **Primary Effect:** When the player initiates a **Picnic**, the Battle-tablecloth can be selected to change the visual pattern of the table. This is purely aesthetic and does not alter the effects of Meal Powers or the quality of sandwiches made.
* **Battle Attributes:**
    * **Fling Power:** N/A (Cannot be used in battle).
    * **Competitive Use:** **None.** This item is strictly for overworld customization and has no impact on Smogon or VGC formats.
* **Evolution/Interaction:** This item does not trigger evolution. Its primary interaction is with the **Picnic Mechanic**, serving as a backdrop for sandwich making, Pokémon bonding, and Egg discovery.

## Acquisition & Lore
* **Availability:** Commonly sold at **Picnic-Knacks** stores located in major Paldean cities such as **Mesagoza**, **Cascarrafa**, and **Levincia**. 
* **Wild Held Item:** This item is never found held by wild Pokémon and cannot be obtained via the **Pickup** ability. It must be purchased or obtained as a reward through specific Paldean NPC interactions or story progression.
