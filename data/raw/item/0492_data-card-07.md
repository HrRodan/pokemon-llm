## Identity & Utility
**Data Card 07** is a **Data Card** introduced in **Generation IV** (specifically appearing in *Pokémon HeartGold and SoulSilver*).
* **Description:** This item serves as a specialized record-keeping tool for the Pokéathlon. It is a **Key Item-adjacent utility** that tracks specific mini-game statistics. It cannot be used in battle or on a Pokémon, and it is not bought with standard currency.
* **Cost:** 0 Poké Dollars (**Not for sale** via traditional shops; acquired via Athlete Points).

## Effect & Mechanics
* **Primary Effect:** Records and displays the number of times the trainer has achieved first place in the **Pennant Capture** event within the Pokéathlon.
* **Battle Attributes:**
    * **Fling Power:** N/A (This item cannot be held or used with the move Fling).
    * **Competitive Use:** **None.** This is a purely cosmetic/completionist item used for tracking records in the Johto region's Pokéathlon Dome. It provides no tactical advantage in trainer battles.
* **Evolution/Interaction:** Does not evolve any Pokémon or interact with battle abilities. It functions exclusively as a data-log for the Pokéathlon Data Card case.

## Acquisition & Lore
* **Availability:** Purchased at the **Pokéathlon Dome** (located north of Goldenrod City) in exchange for **500 Athlete Points (AP)**. It is one of 27 collectible cards used to fill the Data Card Case.
* **Wild Held Item:** Not found on wild Pokémon. It is a unique prize/utility item associated with the physical performance of a trainer's Pokémon team in the Pennant Capture event, a game focused on speed and agility.
