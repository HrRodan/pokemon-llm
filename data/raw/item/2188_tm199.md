## Identity & Utility
**TM199** is a **Technical Machine (TM)** introduced in **Generation IX (Pokémon Scarlet and Violet)**, specifically appearing within the **The Teal Mask (Kitakami)** expansion.
* **Description:** This TM teaches the move **Lash Out** to a compatible Pokémon. As a Generation IX TM, it is a single-use consumable that can be crafted repeatedly using the TM Machine at Pokémon Centers.
* **Cost:** 0 Poké Dollars (**Special Acquisition**). In the Paldea/Kitakami region economy, it is not purchased with standard currency but crafted using **3,000 LP (League Points)**, **3 Mankey Fur**, and **3 Galarian Meowth Fur**.

## Effect & Mechanics
* **Primary Effect:** Teaches the move **Lash Out**. 
    * **Move Type:** Dark
    * **Category:** Physical
    * **Power:** 75 (Doubles to **150** if the user’s stats were lowered during the turn).
    * **Accuracy:** 100%
* **Battle Attributes:**
    * **Fling Power:** N/A (Technical Machines cannot be used with the move Fling in current generations).
    * **Competitive Use:** This is a high-utility **counter-meta move**. It is specifically designed to punish opponents who utilize the **Intimidate** ability or moves like *Icy Wind* and *Snarl*. It is frequently seen on bulky physical attackers (e.g., Kingambit, Tyranitar, or Incineroar) to turn an opponent's debuff into a massive offensive advantage.
* **Evolution/Interaction:** Does not trigger evolution. It is compatible with a wide variety of Physical attackers, particularly those with aggressive or "angry" lore themes (e.g., Primeape, Annihilape, Mabosstiff).

## Acquisition & Lore
* **Availability:** 
    * **Crafting:** Available at any TM Machine once the recipe is unlocked.
    * **Location:** Can be found as a collectible item on the ground in the **Kitakami** region (The Teal Mask DLC), typically in the **Timeless Woods** or awarded by NPCs for progress.
* **Wild Held Item:** Not held by wild Pokémon. Materials required for its creation are gathered by defeating or catching members of the **Mankey** and **Meowth (Galarian)** evolutionary lines.
