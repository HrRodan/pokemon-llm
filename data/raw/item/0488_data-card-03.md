## Identity & Utility
**Data Card 03** is a **Data Card** introduced in **Generation IV** (specifically appearing in *Pokémon HeartGold and SoulSilver*).
* **Description:** It is a specialized record-keeping device used to track specific performance metrics within the Pokéathlon. This card focuses exclusively on the "Dash" mechanic. It is a **Specialty/Key Item** that cannot be bought with standard Poké Dollars and cannot be held by Pokémon in battle.
* **Cost:** 0 Poké Dollars (**Not for sale** via traditional shops; acquired through specialized mini-game currency).

## Effect & Mechanics
* **Primary Effect:** When registered or viewed at the Pokéathlon Dome, it displays the cumulative number of times the trainer’s Pokémon have performed a "dash" during Pokéathlon competitions. This is a tracking mechanic for completionists and those seeking to maximize their Pokéathlon records.
* **Battle Attributes:**
    * **Fling Power:** N/A (This item cannot be held or used in a battle sequence).
    * **Competitive Use:** **None.** This item exists solely for the Pokéathlon sub-game and has no impact on Smogon, VGC, or standard trainer battles.
* **Evolution/Interaction:** This item does not trigger evolution or interact with any Pokémon abilities (such as Pickup or Klutz). It serves purely as a data-logging tool.

## Acquisition & Lore
* **Availability:** This item is obtained at the **Pokéathlon Dome** (located north of Goldenrod City in Johto). It is typically purchased from the Data Card shop using **Athlete Points (AP)**, which are earned by participating in Pokéathlon events.
* **Wild Held Item:** This item is never found on wild Pokémon. It is a unique piece of technology specific to the Johto region's athletic competitions.
* **World Knowledge:** In the context of the Pokéathlon, "dashing" is a vital mechanic in events like the **Hurdle Dash** and **Pennant Race**. Data Card 03 is part of a series of 27 cards that allow players to archive their historical performance across various physical disciplines (Speed, Power, Skill, Stamina, and Jump).
