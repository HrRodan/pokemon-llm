## Identity & Utility
**Polka-Dot Cup** is a picnic customization item introduced in **Generation IX (Scarlet/Violet)**.
* **Description:** A decorative accessory used to personalize the player's picnic set. It is a cosmetic item used within the Paldean picnic mechanic to change the visual theme of the table. It is a reusable aesthetic item that can be purchased.
* **Cost:** 800 Poké Dollars (Cheap/Accessible).

## Effect & Mechanics
* **Primary Effect:** When selected in the picnic customization menu, it replaces the default cups on the picnic table with the Polka-Dot variant. This serves to enhance the "Picnic" experience, which is used for breeding Pokémon (Eggs) and gaining Meal Powers via sandwiches.
* **Battle Attributes:**
    * **Fling Power:** N/A (This item cannot be held in battle and has no Fling power or effect).
    * **Competitive Use:** None. This item is strictly for overworld utility and aesthetic customization; it provides no combat advantages or statistical boosts.
* **Evolution/Interaction:** Does not trigger evolution. Its primary interaction is with the **Picnic System** menu in the Paldea region.

## Acquisition & Lore
* **Availability:** Available for purchase at **Picnic-Knacks** shops located in major Paldean hubs such as Mesagoza, Cascarrafa, and Levincia. 
* **Wild Held Item:** Not found on wild Pokémon. It is a manufactured consumer good intended for trainers and campers. Unlike berries or stones, it cannot be gathered via the **Pickup** ability.
