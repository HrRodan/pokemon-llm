## Identity & Mechanics
**X-Scissor** is a Physical Bug-type move introduced in Generation IV.
* **Description:** The user crosses its scythes, claws, or sharp appendages to deliver a swift, powerful slashing attack in the shape of an "X." This move is characterized by its clinical precision and raw cutting force, often utilized by insectoid or bladed Pokémon to rend through the opponent's defenses.
* **Effect:** Inflicts regular damage with no additional secondary effects.
* **Secondary Effect:** None (0)% chance.
* **Targeting:** Single Target.

## Competitive Profile
* **Stats:** Power 80, Accuracy 100%, PP 15 (Max 24).
* **Role:** Reliable STAB / Consistency Option.
* **Usage:** X-Scissor serves as the standard, reliable Physical Bug-type STAB (Same Type Attack Bonus) for many attackers. While it lacks the utility of **U-turn** or the sheer power of **Megahorn** (Power 120), its perfect accuracy makes it a safe choice for sweeping. In Generation IX, it gained significant value for users with the **Sharpness** ability, which boosts its power by 50%, effectively turning it into a 120-base power move with no drawbacks. It deals **Super Effective** damage to **Grass, Psychic, and Dark** types.
* **Attributes:** This is a **Contact** move, meaning it triggers effects like Rough Skin, Rocky Helmet, or Effect Spore. It is also classified as a **Slicing** move, benefiting from the Sharpness ability.

## Distribution & Notable Users
It has a **Wide** distribution (139 learners), making it one of the most accessible Bug-type moves in the franchise.
* **Prominent Users:** Frequently utilized by **Scizor**, **Pinsir**, and **Golisopod**. It is a cornerstone move for **Kleavor** and **Samurott-Hisui**, both of whom capitalize on the Sharpness boost.
* **Coverage Utility:** Often seen on non-Bug Pokémon such as **Haxorus**, **Gliscor**, and **Terrakion** to provide vital coverage against Psychic and Dark-type threats that might otherwise wall their primary STAB options.
* **Acquisition:** Widely available via **TM/TR** in almost every generation since its debut, ensuring easy access for most clawed or bladed physical attackers.
