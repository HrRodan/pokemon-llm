## Identity & Mechanics
**Snarl** is a Special Dark-type move introduced in Generation V (Unova).
* **Description:** The user unleashes a barrage of aggressive, demoralizing shouts and rants. This auditory assault creates a wave of negative energy that vibrates through the battlefield, suppressing the fighting spirit and concentration of those who hear it.
* **Effect:** Inflicts damage and guaranteed to lower the target's Special Attack stat by one stage.
* **Secondary Effect:** 100% chance to lower Special Attack.
* **Targeting:** Hits all adjacent opponents (Spread Move).

## Competitive Profile
* **Stats:** Power 55, Accuracy 95%, PP 15.
* **Role:** **Utility Debuffer** / **Defensive Support**.
* **Usage:** Snarl is a cornerstone of the VGC (Doubles) format. While its damage output is low, its ability to hit multiple targets and guaranteed Special Attack reduction makes it a premiere tool for neutering special attackers. It is often chosen over more powerful moves like *Dark Pulse* on defensive or supportive Pokémon to increase the longevity of the user's team. It deals **Super Effective** damage to **Psychic** and **Ghost** types.
* **Attributes:** **Sound-based move** (bypasses *Substitute* but is blocked by the *Soundproof* ability). It is a non-contact move.

## Distribution & Notable Users
It has a **Wide** distribution (180 learners), making it one of the most accessible utility moves for Dark, Fire, and Electric types.
* **Prominent Users:** Commonly utilized by **Incineroar**, **Umbreon**, **Arcanine** (both forms), and **Suicune**. It is a signature disruption tool for **Yveltal** in Restricted formats.
* **Coverage Utility:** Frequently seen on non-Dark Pokémon like **Zacian-Crowned**, **Toxtricity**, and **Entei** to provide defensive utility or to punish Ghost-type switch-ins like Calyrex-Shadow.
* **Acquisition:** Widely available via **TM/TR** in most generations, including its origin in Gen V and its continued presence in *Scarlet & Violet*.
