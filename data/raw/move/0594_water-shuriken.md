## Identity & Mechanics
**Water Shuriken** is a Special Water-type move introduced in Generation VI (Kalos).
* **Description:** The user forms and throws rapid-fire throwing stars made of compressed liquid. This ninja-themed technique strikes with extreme speed, slicing through the air to hit the target multiple times before they can react.
* **Effect:** This is a multi-hit move that strikes the target **2 to 5 times** in a single turn. It also possesses a **Priority of +1**, allowing the user to outpace faster opponents.
* **Secondary Effect:** As a multi-hit move, it excels at breaking through **Focus Sashes**, **Sturdy**, and **Substitutes**.
* **Targeting:** Selected Single Pokémon.

## Competitive Profile
* **Stats:** Power 15 (per hit), Accuracy 100%, PP 20.
* **Role:** Priority Finisher / Focus Sash Breaker.
* **Usage:** While its base power per hit is low, the cumulative damage ranges from 30 to 75 (averaging 45). Its primary value lies in being the rare **Special Priority** move, making it an excellent tool for Special Attackers to pick off weakened foes. It deals **Super Effective** damage to **Fire, Ground, and Rock** types. 
* **Attributes:** This move is **not** a contact move, protecting the user from effects like Rough Skin or Rocky Helmet. It is significantly enhanced by the Ability **Battle Bond** (Ash-Greninja), which increases its power and guarantees it hits 3 times.

## Distribution & Notable Users
It has a **Narrow** distribution (4 learners), functioning as a signature or thematic move for ninja-styled Pokémon.
* **Prominent Users:** Almost exclusively associated with **Greninja** (including its Battle Bond and Ash forms), where it serves as a staple priority option.
* **Coverage Utility:** Also learned by **Accelgor**, providing the bug-type ninja with rare priority and niche Water-type coverage to hit Fire and Rock types.
* **Acquisition:** This move is **not available via TM or TR**; it is learned exclusively through leveling up or Move Relearning by its specific users.
