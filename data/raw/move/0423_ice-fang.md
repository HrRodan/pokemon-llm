## Identity & Mechanics
**Ice Fang** is a physical Ice-type move introduced in Generation IV (Sinnoh region).
* **Description:** The user clamps down on the target with fangs infused with sub-zero temperatures. This icy bite delivers immediate physical damage while chilling the target to the bone, potentially leaving them incapacitated by frost or shock. It is categorized as a "biting" move, benefiting from specific abilities like Strong Jaw.
* **Effect:** Inflicts physical damage. It features a unique dual-effect mechanic where it has two independent secondary effect rolls.
* **Secondary Effect:** 10% chance to **Freeze** the target and a separate 10% chance to make the target **Flinch**.
* **Targeting:** Selected single opponent.

## Competitive Profile
* **Stats:** Power 65, Accuracy 95%, PP 15 (Max 24).
* **Role:** Physical Coverage / Tech Move.
* **Usage:** Ice Fang is primarily used as a vital coverage option for physical attackers that lack access to stronger Ice-type moves like *Ice Punch* or *Icicle Crash*. While its base power is relatively low, its ability to hit 4x weaknesses on top-tier threats makes it a common "bracket" move. It deals **Super Effective** damage to **Dragon, Flying, Ground, and Grass** types.
* **Attributes:** This is a **Contact** move and a **Biting** move. It is significantly boosted (50%) by the ability **Strong Jaw**, bringing its effective power to 97.5, surpassing Ice Punch.

## Distribution & Notable Users
It has a **Wide** distribution (123 learners), making it one of the most common physical Ice coverage options for non-Ice types.
* **Prominent Users:** Frequently utilized by **Baxcalibur**, **Gyarados**, and **Mamoswine**. It is also a staple on **Dracovish** and **Mega Sharpedo**, where the *Strong Jaw* ability transforms it into a high-powered threat.
