## Identity & Mechanics
**Psychic** is a Special Psychic-type move introduced in Generation I.
* **Description:** The user leverages a powerful telekinetic force to strike the target. It manifests as a concentrated burst of mental energy that can destabilize the opponent's spiritual and physical defenses. This move is the gold standard for psychic-based offense, representing a pure application of telepathy and psychokinesis.
* **Effect:** Inflicts standard special damage to a single target.
* **Secondary Effect:** Has a **10%** chance to lower the target’s **Special Defense** by one stage.
* **Targeting:** Single Target.

## Competitive Profile
* **Stats:** Power 90, Accuracy 100%, PP 10 (Max 16).
* **Role:** **Reliable STAB** / **Consistency Option**.
* **Usage:** Psychic is the most common offensive Psychic-type move in competitive play due to its perfect accuracy and solid base power. While **Psyshock** is often preferred to hit specially defensive walls (by calculating damage based on physical Defense), Psychic is the superior choice for raw damage against general targets. It deals **Super Effective** damage to **Fighting** and **Poison** types.
* **Attributes:** This move is a non-contact, special projectile. It is not blocked by most common defensive abilities like Bulletproof.

## Distribution & Notable Users
It has a **Wide** distribution (318 learners), making it one of the most accessible Special moves in the franchise.
* **Prominent Users:** Iconic STAB users include **Alakazam**, **Mewtwo**, **Gardevoir**, **Tapu Lele**, and **Latios**.
* **Coverage Utility:** Frequently seen on non-Psychic Pokémon such as **Gengar**, **Lucario**, and **Volcarona** to provide critical coverage against Poison-type threats or to hit neutral targets with high base power.
* **Acquisition:** Extremely widely available; it has been a staple **TM** (TM29 in many games) or **TR** in every single generation since the series' inception.
