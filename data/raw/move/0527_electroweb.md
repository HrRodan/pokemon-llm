## Identity & Mechanics
**Electroweb** is a Special Electric-type move introduced in Generation V (Unova).
* **Description:** The user weaves and launches a shimmering lattice of electrified silk or energy, ensnaring the opposition in a crackling net. This move combines light chip damage with potent crowd control, physically hindering the movements of targets to ensure the user's team gains the upper hand in positioning.
* **Effect:** Deals damage and guaranteed to lower the target's Speed stat by one stage.
* **Secondary Effect:** 100% chance to lower Speed.
* **Targeting:** All Opponents (Spread move; particularly effective in Double Battles).

## Competitive Profile
* **Stats:** Power 55, Accuracy 95%, PP 15.
* **Role:** Speed Control / Utility Support.
* **Usage:** Electroweb is a staple in VGC (Doubles) formats as a method of **Speed Control**. While its base power is low, its ability to hit both opponents and simultaneously lower their speed makes it a tactical alternative to *Icy Wind*. In Singles, it is often used by pivots to slow down an incoming check, allowing a teammate to outspeed them on the following turn. It deals **Super Effective** damage to **Flying** and **Water** types.
* **Attributes:** As a spread move, its damage is reduced by 25% in Double Battles, but it remains one of the few Electric-type utility moves that provides guaranteed stat modification without relying on the RNG of Paralysis.
