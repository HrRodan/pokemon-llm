## Identity & Lore
Mr. Mime (Pokedex #122) is the humanoid Barrier Pok√©mon introduced in Generation I (Kanto). Renowned for its pantomiming ability, this Psychic/Fairy-type creates invisible walls by vibrating its fingertips at high speeds to solidify air particles. While primarily found in urban settings, it is extremely protective of its performance; according to lore, it will aggressively slap anyone who interrupts its act with its broad hands. Though this profile details the standard Kantonian form, a regional **Galarian Form** (Ice/Psychic) exists which specializes in tap dancing rather than pantomime.

## Combat Profile
*   **Stats:** HP 40, Attack 45, Defense 65, Sp. Atk 100, Sp. Def 120, Speed 90.
*   **Archetype:** Special Wall / Fast Utility Support.
*   **Type Matchups:** As a dual Psychic/Fairy-type, it is **weak to Ghost, Poison, and Steel**. It possesses an **immunity to Dragon** and a **double resistance (0.25x) to Fighting**. It also resists Psychic-type attacks.
*   **Move Selection:** Utilizing its high Special Attack and natural utility, optimal moves include **Psychic** and **Dazzling Gleam** for STAB (Same Type Attack Bonus) damage, alongside **Encore** to lock opponents into unfavorable moves, and **Reflect** (or Light Screen) to leverage its "Barrier" lore for team defense.
*   **Abilities:**
    *   **Soundproof:** Grants immunity to all sound-based moves (e.g., Hyper Voice, Roar, Perish Song).
    *   **Filter:** Reduces the damage taken from Super Effective attacks by 25%, significantly improving its survivability.
    *   **Technician (Hidden):** Increases the power of moves with a base power of 60 or less by 50%.

## Evolution & Biology
Mr. Mime is the second stage of a two-stage evolutionary line (three in the Galar region). It evolves from **Mime Jr.** when leveled up while knowing the move **Mimic**. It belongs to the **Human-Like** Egg Group. While the standard Kantonian Mr. Mime does not evolve further, the regional Galarian Mr. Mime is capable of evolving into **Mr. Rime** at Level 42.
