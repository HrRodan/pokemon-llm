## Identity & Lore
Mega Banette (Pokedex #10056) is the Marionette Pok√©mon, representing a tragic and vengeful evolution of a discarded plush doll. Originally introduced in Generation III (Hoenn), this Ghost-type reaches its peak form through Mega Evolution, a transformation that causes the zippers across its body to open, releasing a terrifying amount of cursed energy that was previously suppressed. Its dark lore suggests that it was a lonely toy brought to life by a powerful grudge against the child who threw it away. It inhabits urban environments, lurking in alleys and dark corners, searching for its original owner while sticking pins into its own body to generate energy for laying powerful curses. While it does not have a Gigantamax or Paradox form, its Mega Evolution is a staple of its identity, enhancing its eerie, puppet-like appearance with elongated, spindly limbs and numerous additional zippers.

## Combat Profile
* **Stats:** HP 64, Attack 165, Defense 75, Sp. Atk 93, Sp. Def 83, Speed 75.
* **Archetype:** Prankster Utility Attacker. Despite its massive Physical Attack, Mega Banette often functions as a disruptor due to its ability.
* **Type Matchups:** It is **weak to Ghost and Dark** types. It possesses significant **resistances to Bug and Poison**, and is completely **immune to Normal and Fighting** moves.
* **Move Selection:** Capitalizing on its massive 165 Base Attack and its STAB (Same Type Attack Bonus), key moves include **Shadow Claw** for high critical hit potential, **Shadow Sneak** for priority finishing, **Knock Off** to remove opponent items, and **Gunk Shot** for high-power coverage against Fairy-types.
* **Abilities:** Prankster (Gives priority to status moves, allowing Mega Banette to use moves like Will-O-Wisp, Taunt, or Thunder Wave before the opponent can move, regardless of speed).

## Evolution & Biology
Mega Banette is the final stage of a two-stage evolutionary line, triggered temporarily in battle when a Banette holds a Banettite Mega Stone. It begins its life as **Shuppet**, which is drawn to feelings of envy and malice. Shuppet evolves into **Banette** starting at **Level 37**. Biologically, Mega Banette is characterized by its zippers, which act as a seal for its spectral essence; should they ever be fully undone, its cursed energy would dissipate. It belongs to the **Amorphous** (Indeterminate) Egg Group and is known for a **Fast** growth rate, allowing it to reach combat maturity quickly. Trainers often find it with a **Spell Tag** held item in the wild, which further boosts its ghostly powers.
