## Identity & Lore
Minccino (Pokedex #572) is the Chinchilla Pok√©mon, a charming and meticulously clean creature first discovered in the Unova region during Generation V. Characterized by its soft gray fur and large ears, Minccino is best known for its obsession with hygiene; it uses its long, broom-like tail to sweep and dust its surroundings and greets others of its kind by rubbing tails together in a grooming ritual. In its natural habitat, if it finds an object that is dirty, it will spend hours cleaning it until it is spotless. While Minccino does not currently possess any Mega Evolutions, Gigantamax forms, or Paradox variants, its evolved form, Cinccino, is renowned in competitive circles for its multi-hit capabilities.

## Combat Profile
* **Stats:** HP 55, Attack 50, Defense 40, Sp. Atk 40, Sp. Def 40, Speed 75.
* **Archetype:** Fast Physical Attacker / Utility Lead.
* **Type Matchups:** It is **weak to Fighting** and possesses a total **immunity to Ghost**. As a pure Normal-type, it has no resistances to other elemental types.
* **Move Selection:** Utilizing its high Speed and access to multi-hit moves, Minccino excels with **Tail Slap** (its signature STAB move), **Triple Axel** (for Ice-type coverage), **Knock Off** (to remove opponent held items), and **U-turn** (to maintain positioning and momentum).
* **Abilities:** **Cute Charm** (may infatuate attackers of the opposite gender upon contact), **Technician** (boosts the power of moves with 60 base power or less by 50%), and its Hidden Ability **Skill Link** (ensures that multi-hit moves like Tail Slap always hit the maximum number of times).

## Evolution & Biology
Minccino is the first stage of a two-stage evolutionary line. It evolves into **Cinccino** when exposed to a **Shiny Stone** at any level. Biologically, it is highly efficient at foraging and belongs to the **Ground (Field)** Egg Group. It has a hatch counter of 15, indicating a relatively fast breeding cycle. In the wild, Minccino are occasionally found carrying a **Chesto Berry**, likely used to quickly recover from sleep-inducing conditions during its constant grooming sessions.
