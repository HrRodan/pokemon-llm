## Identity & Lore
Paldean Wooper (Pokedex #10253) is the Poison/Ground-type Paldean Form of the Water Fish Pok√©mon, originally discovered in the Johto region but adapted to the Paldea region in Generation IX. Unlike its Johto counterpart, Paldean Wooper migrated to land after losing territory battles underwater. To survive on land, it developed a poisonous film to protect its body, giving it a duller brown color compared to the classic blue variant. Its gills have hardened into thick, bone-like structures. While it lacks a Mega Evolution or Gigantamax form, its evolution, Clodsire, is a prominent defensive staple in the Paldean ecosystem. It thrives in muddy wetlands and "waters-edge" habitats where it searches for food in cold weather.

## Combat Profile
* **Stats:** HP 55, Attack 45, Defense 45, Sp. Atk 25, Sp. Def 25, Speed 15.
* **Archetype:** Defensive Hazard Setter / Physical Utility.
* **Type Matchups:** It is **weak to Ground, Psychic, Water, Grass, and Ice**. It possesses a crucial **immunity to Electric** moves and resists Poison, Rock, Fairy, Bug, and Fighting-type attacks.
* **Move Selection:** Utilizing its superior Attack and massive utility pool, Paldean Wooper excels with **Earthquake** (high-power Ground STAB), **Poison Jab** (reliable Poison STAB), **Recover** (essential longevity), and **Toxic Spikes** (entry hazard pressure).
* **Abilities:** 
    * **Poison Point:** May poison attackers that make direct contact, punishing physical sweepers.
    * **Water Absorb:** Grants immunity to Water-type moves and restores HP when hit by them, effectively neutralizing one of its primary weaknesses.
    * **Unaware (Hidden Ability):** A top-tier competitive ability that allows Wooper to ignore the opponent's stat changes when taking or dealing damage.

## Evolution & Biology
Paldean Wooper is the first stage of a two-stage evolutionary line. It evolves into **Clodsire** starting at **Level 20**. This is a distinct evolutionary branch compared to the Johto variant, which evolves into Quagsire at the same level. Biologically, Paldean Wooper is highly resilient, belonging to the **Water 1 and Ground** Egg Groups. With a hatch counter of 20, it is a common sight for breeders looking to produce defensive walls. Its slow speed is compensated by its ability to stall opponents using its toxic skin and durable earthen biology.
