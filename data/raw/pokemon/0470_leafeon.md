## Identity & Lore
Leafeon (Pokedex #470) is the Verdant Pok√©mon introduced in Generation IV (Sinnoh). A mammalian quadruped with a cellular structure closer to plants than animals, Leafeon is capable of performing photosynthesis, which results in it being constantly surrounded by clean, crisp air. While it possesses a pacified nature, preferring to sunbathe rather than fight, its tail is razor-sharp and can slice through large trees. It is one of the eight distinct evolutions of Eevee.

## Combat Profile
*   **Stats:** HP 65, Attack 110, Defense 130, Sp. Atk 60, Sp. Def 65, Speed 95.
*   **Archetype:** Physical Tank / Sun Sweeper.
*   **Type Matchups:** As a pure **Grass-type**, it is **weak to Fire, Ice, Poison, Flying, and Bug**. It **resists Water, Grass, Electric, and Ground**.
*   **Move Selection:** Capitalizing on its high Attack and Defense, key moves include **Leaf Blade** (High Crit STAB), **Swords Dance** (Attack scaling), **Synthesis** (Recovery, enhanced in sun), and **Solar Blade** (High damage output in sun).
*   **Abilities:** **Leaf Guard** (Prevents status conditions in harsh sunlight) and the Hidden Ability **Chlorophyll** (Doubles Speed during harsh sunlight), making it a premier threat on Sun teams.

## Evolution & Biology
Leafeon is a stage-1 evolution in the branched Eevee family. It evolves from **Eevee** via two distinct methods depending on the game generation: historically by leveling up near a **Moss Rock** (found in locations like Eterna Forest or Pinwheel Forest), or in modern generations by being exposed to a **Leaf Stone**. It belongs to the **Field** (Ground) Egg Group.
