## Identity & Lore
Ultra Necrozma (Pokedex #10157) is the Prism Pok√©mon, a terrifyingly powerful life-form that represents the true, radiant form of Necrozma after it has absorbed an immense amount of light energy. Appearing in Generation VII (Alola), this entity is legendary across the Ultra Space dimensions and is often referred to in ancient Alolan myths as "The Blinding One." While its base form is a fractured, black crystalline being, this Ultra form is a golden, draconic manifestation of pure light, reaching a staggering height of 7.5 meters. Historically, it is believed to have shared its light with the Alola region in the distant past before losing its power and falling into a deep slumber underground. This form is uniquely triggered through a process called "Ultra Burst," which occurs when Necrozma is fused with either Solgaleo (Dusk Mane Necrozma) or Lunala (Dawn Wings Necrozma) and utilizes the power of a specific Z-Crystal, the Ultranecrozium Z.

## Combat Profile
* **Stats:** HP 97, Attack 167, Defense 97, Sp. Atk 167, Sp. Def 97, Speed 129.
* **Archetype:** Uber-Tier Mixed Sweeper. With equal, monstrously high offensive stats and a blistering speed tier, it can dismantle teams from both the physical and special sides.
* **Type Matchups:** It is a **Psychic/Dragon** type. It is **weak to Bug, Ghost, Dark, Ice, Dragon, and Fairy** types. It **resists Fighting, Poison, Fire, Water, Grass, and Electric** types.
* **Move Selection:** Ultra Necrozma utilizes its signature light-based arsenal to overwhelm opponents. Key moves include **Photon Geyser** (a category-shifting move that hits based on the higher offensive stat), **Prismatic Laser** (a devastating special nuke), **Dragon Pulse** (consistent special STAB), and **Earth Power** (critical coverage for Steel-type threats). 
* **Abilities:** Neuroforce (This ability increases the damage of super-effective attacks by 20%, making it nearly impossible to switch into safely if the opponent has a type weakness).

## Evolution & Biology
Ultra Necrozma is the final, temporary pinnacle of a complex transformation line rather than a traditional evolutionary chain. It originates from **Necrozma**, which does not evolve but can undergo "Fusion" with the mascots of the Alola region. When Necrozma uses the N-Solarizer to fuse with Solgaleo, it becomes **Dusk Mane Necrozma**; when it uses the N-Lunarizer to fuse with Lunala, it becomes **Dawn Wings Necrozma**. Only from these fused states can it undergo the **Ultra Burst** transformation mid-battle to reach its Ultra form. Biologically, it is an extraterrestrial organism composed of light-refracting crystal. It belongs to the **No Eggs Discovered** egg group, meaning it cannot breed, and it possesses a slow growth rate, reflecting the immense energy required to sustain its cosmic power.
