## Identity & Lore
Blaziken (Pokédex #257) is the Blaze Pokémon, a dual-type Fire/Fighting starter native to Generation III (Hoenn). Standing at 1.9 meters with a primarily red, upright physique, this Pokémon is capable of blowing intense flames from its wrists, incinerating opponents with courageous fervor. Its flames burn more vigorously when it faces a powerful opponent, highlighting its competitive warrior spirit. While primarily found in grassland habitats, Blaziken is renowned for its immense martial prowess and ability to clear 30-story buildings with a single leap. It possesses a **Mega Evolution** (Mega Blaziken) and is often cited as the progenitor of the Fire/Fighting starter archetype.

## Combat Profile
*   **Stats:** HP 80, Attack 120, Defense 70, Sp. Atk 110, Sp. Def 70, Speed 80.
*   **Archetype:** Mixed Setup Sweeper.
*   **Type Matchups:** It is **weak to Flying, Ground, Water, Psychic, and Fairy**, and **resists Bug, Steel, Fire, Grass, Ice, and Dark**.
*   **Move Selection:** Capitalizing on its high Attack and Special Attack, key moves include **Flare Blitz** (Fire STAB), **Close Combat** (Fighting STAB), **Swords Dance** (to sharply raise Attack), and **Protect** (to safely accumulate Speed boosts). Other viable options include Brave Bird and Thunder Punch.
*   **Abilities:**
    *   **Blaze:** Powers up Fire-type moves when the Pokémon's HP is low.
    *   **Speed Boost (Hidden Ability):** Its Speed stat is gradually boosted at the end of every turn, making it a threatening "snowball" sweeper.

## Evolution & Biology
Blaziken is the final form of a 3-stage evolutionary line. It evolves from **Combusken** starting at **Level 36**, which evolves from **Torchic** starting at **Level 16**. It belongs to the **Ground** Egg Group and possesses a hatch counter of 20. Biologically, its feathers burn off and regrow annually, keeping its plumage fresh for combat.
