## Identity & Lore
Linoone (Pokedex #264) is the Normal-type "Rushing Pok√©mon" introduced in Generation III (Hoenn). Characterized by its sleek, aerodynamic body and white fur, Linoone is notorious for its inability to run in anything but a straight line. According to ecological data, it dashes at full speed to catch prey but faces significant challenges on curving roads, forcing it to make sharp, right-angle turns to evade obstacles. While this profile details the standard Hoennian form found in grasslands, a Dark/Normal regional variant exists in the Galar region. Linoone is often observed carrying items like Potions or Berries, reflecting its scavenging nature.

## Combat Profile
*   **Stats:** HP 78, Attack 70, Defense 61, Sp. Atk 50, Sp. Def 61, Speed 100.
*   **Archetype:** Fast Setup Sweeper.
*   **Type Matchups:** It is **weak to Fighting** and possesses an **immunity to Ghost**. It has no natural resistances.
*   **Move Selection:** While its base Attack is modest, Linoone becomes a dangerous threat after setting up. Its optimal physical kit includes:
    *   **Belly Drum:** Maximizes Attack stat in exchange for HP, turning Linoone into a sweeper.
    *   **Double-Edge:** A high-power STAB (Same Type Attack Bonus) move to capitalize on the attack boost.
    *   **Shadow Claw:** Provides essential coverage against Ghost-types that are immune to its Normal attacks.
    *   **Play Rough:** Offers coverage against Fighting-types which threaten Linoone.
*   **Abilities:**
    *   **Pickup:** A utility ability that allows Linoone to find items after battle; highly valued during adventure gameplay.
    *   **Gluttony:** The preferred competitive ability, allowing Linoone to consume held Berries at 50% HP rather than 25% (synergizing perfectly with Belly Drum).
    *   **Quick Feet (Hidden):** Increases Speed by 50% if Linoone is afflicted with a status condition.

## Evolution & Biology
Linoone is part of a 2-stage evolutionary line in the Hoenn region, though its lineage extends to a third stage in Galar. It evolves from **Zigzagoon** starting at **Level 20**. It belongs to the **Ground** Egg Group.
*   **Regional Note:** While the provided data references an evolution into Obstagoon, this is exclusive to the **Galarian Linoone**. The standard Normal-type Linoone profiled here represents the final stage of its specific line and cannot evolve further.
