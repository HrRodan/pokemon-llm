## Identity & Lore
Lileep (Pokedex #345) is the **Sea Lily Pok√©mon**, a prehistoric species originally discovered in the **Hoenn** region (Generation III). Resurrected from the **Root Fossil**, this ancient creature became extinct approximately 100 million years ago. Despite its plant-like appearance, Lileep is distinctively carnivorous; it anchors itself firmly to rocks on the seafloor using strong tentacles to avoid drifting in currents. It behaves like a crinoid, waving its flower-petal-shaped tentacles to disguise itself as aquatic flora, waiting to ensnare approaching prey to swallow whole.

## Combat Profile
*   **Stats:** HP 66, Attack 41, Defense 77, Sp. Atk 61, Sp. Def 87, Speed 23.
*   **Archetype:** Special Tank / Defensive Anchor.
*   **Type Matchups:** As a unique **Rock/Grass** type, it is **weak to Ice, Fighting, Bug, and Steel**. It **resists Normal and Electric** attacks.
*   **Move Selection:** Lileep excels as a stalling unit by leveraging its bulk and recovery options. Key moves include **Recover** for reliable healing, **Giga Drain** for STAB damage and health regeneration, **Ancient Power** for Rock-type coverage with a chance to boost stats, and **Stealth Rock** to set entry hazards.
*   **Abilities:**
    *   **Suction Cups:** Negates moves that force switching (like Roar or Whirlwind), fitting its lore as a seafloor anchor.
    *   **Storm Drain (Hidden):** Draws in all Water-type moves to raise Special Attack, effectively granting Lileep an **immunity** to a type that would usually damage Rock-types.

## Evolution & Biology
Lileep is the first stage of a two-stage evolutionary line. It evolves into the formidable **Cradily** when leveled up to **Level 40**. Biologically, it belongs to the **Water 3** Egg Group, reflecting its aquatic invertebrate nature.
