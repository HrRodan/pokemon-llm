## Identity & Lore
Altaria (Pokedex #334) is the Dragon/Flying-type Humming Pok√©mon introduced in Generation III (Hoenn). Residing primarily in forests and mountainous altitudes, this avian dragon mimics billowing, cotton-like clouds to camouflage itself while wheeling through the sky. Famous for its crystal-clear soprano voice, Altaria sings melodies that induce a sense of dreamy wonderment in its listeners. While typically Blue and white in coloration, it possesses a powerful transformation known as **Mega Altaria**, capable of occurring when holding an Altarianite, which changes its typing to Dragon/Fairy and significantly boosts its offensive and defensive capabilities.

## Combat Profile
*   **Stats:** HP 75, Attack 70, Defense 90, Sp. Atk 70, Sp. Def 105, Speed 80.
*   **Archetype:** Special Defensive Tank / Setup Sweeper.
*   **Type Matchups:** As a Dragon/Flying type, it suffers a critical **Double Weakness (4x) to Ice** moves. It is also weak to Rock, Dragon, and Fairy types. However, it boasts an immunity to Ground types and resists Fighting, Bug, Fire, Water, and a double resistance to Grass.
*   **Move Selection:** Capitalizing on its high Special Defense and support capabilities, key moves include **Cotton Guard** (drastically raising Defense), **Roost** (for reliable longevity), **Moonblast** (providing excellent coverage against Dragon-types), and **Dragon Pulse** (reliable STAB damage).
*   **Abilities:**
    *   **Natural Cure:** Heals all status conditions (burn, paralysis, etc.) upon switching out, making it an excellent pivot against status-heavy teams.
    *   **Cloud Nine (Hidden):** Eliminates the effects of weather (such as Rain or Sandstorm) while on the field.

## Evolution & Biology
Altaria is part of a 2-stage evolutionary line. It evolves from **Swablu** starting at **Level 35**. It belongs to the **Flying** and **Dragon** Egg Groups and has a unique "slow-then-very-fast" growth rate. While its pre-evolution acts as a cleaner of surroundings, Altaria embraces a more nomadic, aerial lifestyle, bonding with Trainers who appreciate its musical qualities.
