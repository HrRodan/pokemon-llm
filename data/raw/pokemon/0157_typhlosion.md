## Identity & Lore
Typhlosion (Pokedex #157) is the Volcano Pok√©mon, serving as the final evolution of the Fire-type starter from **Generation II (Johto)**. Characterized by its explosive temperament, this quadrupedal beast possesses a yellow and dark blue coloration and typically inhabits grasslands. According to lore, when its rage peaks, its body temperature rises to such extremes that anything touching it instantly bursts into flames. It has the ability to create massive explosions by rubbing its blazing fur together to generate kinetic heat. While the standard form is pure Fire-type, **World Knowledge** dictates the existence of a regional variant, **Hisuian Typhlosion**, which is a Fire/Ghost type native to the ancient Hisui region.

## Combat Profile
*   **Stats:** HP 78, Attack 84, Defense 78, Sp. Atk 109, Sp. Def 85, Speed 100.
*   **Archetype:** Fast Special Sweeper.
*   **Type Matchups:** As a pure Fire-type, it is **weak to Ground, Rock, and Water**. It **resists Bug, Steel, Grass, Ice, Fairy, and Fire** attacks.
*   **Move Selection:** Capitalizing on its high Speed and Special Attack, its most viable moveset includes **Eruption** (massive damage at full HP), **Fire Blast** or **Flamethrower** (reliable STAB), **Focus Blast** (coverage against Rock types), and **Solar Beam** (coverage against Water/Ground/Rock, ideally used with Sunny Day support).
*   **Abilities:**
    *   **Blaze:** Increases the power of Fire-type moves by 50% when HP is below 1/3.
    *   **Flash Fire (Hidden Ability):** Grants immunity to Fire-type moves and boosts the power of its own Fire moves by 50% when hit by one.

## Evolution & Biology
Typhlosion is the final stage of a 3-stage evolutionary line. It evolves from **Quilava** starting at **Level 36**, which in turn evolves from **Cyndaquil** starting at **Level 14**. It belongs to the **Ground (Field)** Egg Group and has a medium-slow growth rate.
