## Identity & Lore
Cacturne (Pokedex #332) is the Scarecrow Pok√©mon, a dual-type Grass/Dark creature introduced in Generation III (Hoenn). Resembling a humanoid cactus with a eerie, scarecrow-like silhouette, Cacturne is perfectly adapted to harsh desert environments. Its biology dictates a nocturnal lifestyle; during the day, it stands completely motionless to conserve moisture under the scorching sun, becoming active only when the temperature drops at night. Lore suggests that Cacturne packs trail travelers through the desert, waiting for them to succumb to exhaustion.

## Combat Profile
*   **Stats:** HP 70, Attack 115, Defense 60, Sp. Atk 115, Sp. Def 60, Speed 55.
*   **Archetype:** Slow Mixed Wallbreaker.
*   **Type Matchups:** Cacturne possesses a debilitating **Double Weakness (4x) to Bug** attacks. It is also weak to **Fighting, Flying, Poison, Fire, Ice, and Fairy**. However, it is **immune to Psychic** attacks and resists **Ground, Ghost, Water, Grass, Electric, and Dark**.
*   **Move Selection:** Capitalizing on its equal and high offensive stats (115 in both Attack and Sp. Atk) while compensating for low speed, key moves include **Sucker Punch** (Priority STAB), **Energy Ball** or **Seed Bomb** (Primary STAB), **Focus Blast** (Coverage against Steel/Ice), and **Spikes** (Hazard setting utility).
*   **Abilities:**
    *   **Sand Veil:** Increases evasion during a sandstorm.
    *   **Water Absorb (Hidden):** Restores HP if hit by a Water-type move, allowing Cacturne to switch safely into Water attacks.

## Evolution & Biology
Cacturne is the final stage of a 2-stage evolutionary line. It evolves from **Cacnea** starting at **Level 32**. Biologically, its blood is composed of sand-like substances, and wild specimens are occasionally found holding a **Poison Barb** or **Sticky Barb**. It belongs to the **Plant** and **Humanshape** Egg Groups.
