## Identity & Lore
Gliscor (Pokédex #472) is the Fang Scorpion Pokémon, a menacing nocturnal predator introduced in Generation IV (Sinnoh). Characterized by its purple exoskeleton and large bat-like wings, this Pokémon hangs inverted from branches to observe its prey before swooping down silently to strike. It captures victims with its powerful pincers and long tail stinger. Gliscor possesses the unique ability to fly soundlessly, allowing it to navigate the night skies with stealth despite its terrifying appearance.

## Combat Profile
*   **Stats:** HP 75, Attack 95, Defense 125, Sp. Atk 45, Sp. Def 75, Speed 95.
*   **Archetype:** Physical Tank / Stallbreaker.
*   **Type Matchups:** It is **weak to Water** and possesses a **Double Weakness (4x) to Ice**. It **resists Fighting, Poison, and Bug** types, and boasts full **immunity to Ground and Electric** attacks.
*   **Move Selection:** Capitalizing on its high Defense and decent Attack, key moves include **Earthquake** (STAB), **Facade** (synergy with Poison Heal), **Roost** (longevity), and **Swords Dance** (setup).
*   **Abilities:**
    *   **Hyper Cutter:** Prevents the Pokémon's Attack stat from being lowered.
    *   **Sand Veil:** Boosts the Pokémon's evasiveness in a sandstorm.
    *   **Poison Heal (Hidden):** Restores HP if the Pokémon is poisoned instead of losing HP, making it tactically superior when holding a Toxic Orb.

## Evolution & Biology
Gliscor is part of a 2-stage evolutionary line. It evolves from **Gligar** when leveled up at **night** while holding a **Razor Fang**. It belongs to the **Bug** Egg Group, requiring approximately 20 cycles to hatch.
