## Identity & Lore
Infernape (Pokedex #392) is the Flame Pokémon introduced in Generation IV (Sinnoh). A dual-type Fire/Fighting creature, it draws significant inspiration from the legend of the Monkey King, Sun Wukong. This bipedal simian is characterized by a crown of fire that burns atop its head, which never extinguishes and intensifies based on its fighting spirit. Infernape utilizes a specialized form of martial arts involving all four of its limbs, granting it exceptional agility and combat prowess. As the final evolution of the Sinnoh region's starter Pokémon Chimchar, it is renowned for its high speed and versatility in battle.

## Combat Profile
*   **Stats:** HP 76, Attack 104, Defense 71, Sp. Atk 104, Sp. Def 71, Speed 108.
*   **Archetype:** Fast Mixed Sweeper / Pivot.
*   **Type Matchups:** It is **weak to Water, Ground, Flying, and Psychic** types. It **resists Fire, Grass, Ice, Bug, Dark, and Steel** types.
*   **Move Selection:** Capitalizing on its equal Attack and Special Attack stats, key moves include **Close Combat** (high-power STAB), **Flare Blitz** or **Fire Blast** (for physical or special damage), **Grass Knot** (coverage against Water/Ground types), and **U-turn** (for pivoting momentum). Other viable options include **Mach Punch** for priority and **Stealth Rock** for hazard setting.
*   **Abilities:**
    *   **Blaze:** Powers up Fire-type moves when the Pokémon's HP is low.
    *   **Iron Fist (Hidden Ability):** Boosts the power of punching moves, enhancing attacks like Thunder Punch, Fire Punch, and Mach Punch.

## Evolution & Biology
Infernape is part of a 3-stage evolutionary line. It evolves from Monferno starting at **Level 36**, which itself evolves from Chimchar at **Level 14**. It belongs to the **Ground** and **Humanshape** Egg Groups. Anatomically, it possesses gold plating on its chest and shoulders, and blue markings on its knees and hands, signifying its progression into a master martial artist.
