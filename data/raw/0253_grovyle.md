## Identity & Lore
Grovyle (Pokedex #253) is the Wood Gecko Pokémon introduced in **Generation III (Hoenn)**. A master of arboreal agility, this Grass-type Pokémon utilizes the long leaves growing from its body for expert camouflage within its natural **Forest** habitat. Known for its incredible speed and climbing ability, Grovyle effortlessly leaps from branch to branch in jungles, often closing in on prey before it can be detected. While it does not possess a form change itself, its final evolution, Sceptile, is capable of **Mega Evolution**. Grovyle holds significant prominence in Pokémon lore as a key protagonist in the *Pokémon Mystery Dungeon* series.

## Combat Profile
*   **Stats:** HP 50, Attack 65, Defense 45, Sp. Atk 85, Sp. Def 65, Speed 95.
*   **Archetype:** Fast Special Sweeper.
*   **Type Matchups:** As a pure Grass-type, it is **weak to Fire, Ice, Poison, Flying, and Bug**. It **resists Water, Electric, Grass, and Ground** moves.
*   **Move Selection:** Capitalizing on its superior Speed and Special Attack, key moves include **Leaf Storm** for massive burst damage, **Energy Ball** for consistent STAB (Same Type Attack Bonus), **Giga Drain** for sustainability, and **Vacuum Wave** for priority damage against faster threats.
*   **Abilities:**
    *   **Overgrow:** Powers up Grass-type moves when the Pokémon's HP is low.
    *   **Unburden (Hidden):** Doubles the Pokémon's Speed stat if its held item is used or lost.

## Evolution & Biology
Grovyle is part of a 3-stage evolutionary line. It evolves from **Treecko** starting at **Level 16** and evolves into **Sceptile** starting at **Level 36**. Biologically, it falls into the **Monster** and **Dragon** Egg Groups, reflecting its reptilian characteristics and compatibility with draconic species.
